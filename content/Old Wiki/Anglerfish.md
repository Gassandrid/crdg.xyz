## Description

![A Close up of the Anglerfish in
game.](Angllear.png "A Close up of the Anglerfish in game.") The
Anglerfish is one of the many fish that can be obtained, it is
characterized by it\'s light bulb and round brown body.

## Function

Upon activation it\'s light bulb activates and begins glowing, emitting
light in a moderate radius.

## Trivia

- In real life, an anglerfish\'s \"light bulb\" is called an esca. It
  can\'t be directly turned off by the anglerfish, though some types of
  anglerfish have a skin flap that can enclose the esca so light can\'t
  escape.
  - This is likely the case for the in-game anglerfish since it can stop
    emitting light, although there is no visible skin on or around the
    esca so it may not be the case.
- Anglerfish use the esca in order to attract prey for them to eat. The
  esca works because of bioluminescent bacteria contained inside of the
  clear membrane.

<!-- -->

- The anglerfish in game is a female anglerfish, male anglerfish are
  extremely small and have no esca.
