The beehive is a natural object in CRDG. It is a sphere with a decal of
bee honeycomb pasted on each side, with bees swirling around it.

## Function

If hit quickly, the beehive will fall off of where it is hanging and
onto the ground. If hit more it will eventually break into 1-3
[honeycombs](Honeycomb "honeycombs"){.wikilink} used in beekeeping, and
releasing the bees. The bees will attack and usually kill everyone
around before flying into the frames, which are now safe to use. The bee
repellent lets you not get killed by the bees, which is useful because
the bees are incredibly violent. They will attempt to kill everyone who
doesn\'t have bee repellent. Since most beehive makers have bee
repellent, they target a random person. The bees are capable of
exploding fuel tanks and jerry cans. There is no escape once they begin
attacking a player.

## Spawning

Beehives have a rather simple algorithm for spawning. The system waits
10-15 minutes before selecting a random location, which then gets a
beehive on the bottom of one the possible spawning places in that
location.
