The Makarov is a gamepass item which is used for mobility or fighting
other players. It appears to be a small gun which is held in both hands
in front of the player.

## Usage

Upon use, the makarov fires a rather inaccurate but fast bullet towards
the cursor. Upon hit it deals some damage, more on headshot. Also knocks
in the opposite direction that their screen is facing, sometimes causing
[Destabilization](Destabilization_system "Destabilization"){.wikilink}.
This can be used for faster movement, as well as being paired with the
[Miracle of
momentum](Miracle_of_momentum "Miracle of momentum"){.wikilink} to fling
very far. It can fire 8 times on a magazine, but is fast to reload. Can
have the magazine swapped, even if full. Has no auto-reload like some
may be used to, it must be reloaded by manually pressing R.

## Obtainment

The Makarov can be purchased for 220 Robux.
