The ball of oblotenium is a component in CRDG. It is a small metal
sphere.

## Function

The ball of oblotenium has no special properties other than it\'s
weight. It is very dense and is hard to lift, if multiple are glued
together a player can\'t get it off the ground. They are primarily used
when a lot of extra weight is needed, such as anchoring batteries at the
cart dealership, however [Spikes](content/Old%20Wiki/Spikes.md "spikes"){.wikilink} are better
for this purpose. They make a great counterweight if you need something
to stay upright or faced a certain way, especially if it is suspended
via ropes or other methods.
