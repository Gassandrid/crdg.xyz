`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the Sea
otter in game.](Not_a_fish_2.png "A close up of the Sea otter in game.")
The Sea otter is the only mammal that can be obtained, it is
characterized by it\'s small flippers and is covered in fur.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

Upon activation it will start moving in circles and do nothing but if
you glue it to a surface and let it have it\'s flippers on cart buttons
then it will start clicking them randomly.(basically like a cart
clicker)
