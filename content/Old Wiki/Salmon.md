### Description

The salmon is one of the many fish that can be obtained, it is quite
common and is characterized by its red body and smallish size.

### Function

![A closeup of a salmon as seen is
game](Salmonnn.png "A closeup of a salmon as seen is game") When
activated the salmon, like most other fish will flop around. The only
difference is that when the activated salmon is moved, it will return to
the location that it was activated at. It will continue to stay or
attempt to return to said location until it is deactivated.
