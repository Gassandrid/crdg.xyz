Rusty Batteries can be found at [Bill\'s
Batteries](Bill's_Batteries "Bill's Batteries"){.wikilink}. They\'re
used for opening the gate at the [Cart
Dealership](Cart_Dealership "Cart Dealership"){.wikilink}. Once placed
into the slot, it will temporarily open the gate, throwing the battery
in to the void, although he battery can be saved by quickly taking it
out putting it behind a wall.

If you don\'t want to go through the hassle of bringing a battery to the
[Cart Dealership](Cart_Dealership "Cart Dealership"){.wikilink}, you can
also buy a premium battery at the Cart Dealership for 20 Robux per
battery.
