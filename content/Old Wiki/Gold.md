![](Gold.png "Gold.png") [Gold](Gold "Gold"){.wikilink} can be found at
the [Gas Station](Gas_Station "Gas Station"){.wikilink}. It is used to
make golden lemonade. It is in a secret room above the shop area of the
Petrol Station and in order to make golden lemonade you must take it to
the furnace room at the [Cathedral](Cathedral "Cathedral"){.wikilink}
and melt it. You must catch the molten gold that spills out in some
planters. Then, put the lemon in a golden planter and wait for the first
lemon to grow. The first lemon will be golden, and if you put it in a
golden planter, it will grow a tree of golden lemons to take to the
Petrol Station to make into lemonade.

A good way to get gold out is either slamming it against the wall, or
attaching two ropes, glitch one inside and glue it to gold, spike the
one outside and shrink it over and over (button on the BLUE side) and it
will just pop out.
