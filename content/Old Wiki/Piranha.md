`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the piranha
in game.](Pirhanna.png "A close up of the piranha in game.") The Piranha
is one of the many fish that can be obtained, it is characterized by
it\'s small blue body and sharp teeth.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

Upon activation the piranha will start flopping away to the nearest
player and upon contact it will start biting. It does 10 damage every
tick or sound cue.It can be used as a
[seeker](Seeker_(Manifest) "seeker"){.wikilink} from the
[manifest](Manifests "manifest"){.wikilink}.
