The Spawn island is the first stage of the game. It has 3 cart spawners,
engine (test) cart, regular cart, and passenger cart. There are 2 spawn
points. If you join the group, you get access to the group rooms with a
pan spawner in it. There\'s also \"buy it\" sections that are used for
gamepasses. There\'s also an [NPC](NPCs "NPC"){.wikilink} called [Crazy
Jim](NPCs#Crazy_Jim "Crazy Jim"){.wikilink} **while in private servers**
that allows you to change modifiers by speaking to him.

Anyone wishing to build anything should move to further areas in order
to avoid their creations being destroyed by the chaotic players at the
spawn

It is the location with the most
[spawnoids](Spawnoid "spawnoids"){.wikilink} due to it being their
natural habitat.
