The nuclear motor is a component produced via the [component
machine](Component_Machine "component machine"){.wikilink}. It appears
to be a rather squat, metal machine with piping attached to it and a
compartment with a blue fluid in it, likely water. The machine does not
leak when turned on it\'s side or top. It has a plate on the side that
spins when the machine is activated.

## Function

The nuclear motor is initially inert until a [uranium
rod](Uranium_Rod "uranium rod"){.wikilink} is inserted into it\'s fuel
compartment. Once fueled, the nuclear motor will start spinning slowly
while playing a chugging sound. Over time, the machine will start
jostling around, so usually the rod will fall out, stopping the machine
if it hasn\'t been glued or stapled in. As the rod is in the slot for
longer, the motor will spin faster and faster and the chugging speeds up
too, as the jostling becomes stronger. Sometimes if it isn\'t anchored
properly the jostling is violent enough that it will seemingly forget
gravity exists and fly off the map, even when it is not near the edge.
Eventually, it starts becoming tinted orange, before exploding
violently. The whole process takes about 20 seconds of the rod being in
the machine.

Unlike other explosives, the nuclear motor releases several separate
explosions in a quick succession when it is triggered. Unlike other
explosives, it can not be triggered via other explosions, and is rather
just destroyed.

## Tips

If you want to use it as a bomb, there are several ways to make sure it
ends up exploding, as well as making it more potent.

- [Glue](Glue "Glue"){.wikilink} or staple the uranium rod in rather
  than just setting it in so it doesn\'t jostle out and stop.
- Use a [spike](content/Old%20Wiki/Spikes.md "spike"){.wikilink} machine to anchor it to the
  ground so people can\'t pick it up and throw it off.
  - remember to remove the button from the spikes so they don\'t just
    press it and throw it away.
- If you want to make the explosion stronger put
  [jerrycans](Jerry_can "jerrycans"){.wikilink} near but not on top of
  it. An easier and more accessible, but riskier method is to put the
  machine by one of the [cart](Carts "cart"){.wikilink} spawning areas.

If you shoot the uranium rod in the middle of the spinning, you can turn
it to a motor and make it not explode. To do this you need to have a
Component machine \"[Gun](content/Old%20Wiki/Gun.md "Gun"){.wikilink}\". Than put the rod in
the motor with the gun glued with it. After a bit press the button/
activate the gun.

This will create a infinite spinning motion (uncontrollable, gets REALLY
fast).
