### Opening note {#opening_note}

This guide details the traps in the game, but does not have information
on winning when there are people trying to stop you. Depending on what
they have and their position, trolls and
[spawnoids](spawnoid "spawnoids"){.wikilink} can range from minor
annoyance to making the game impossible. If this happens, you should
probably change server.

Also note that this guide is intended to be performed with a standard
cart and single passenger cart. Anything more significantly complicates
moving and makes certain things impossible to do.

### [Spawn](Spawn "Spawn"){.wikilink} to Bill\'s Batteries {#spawn_to_bills_batteries}

![A close up of Spawn.](Spawnling.png "A close up of Spawn.") Getting to
Bill\'s Batteries is very simple. You just need to ride the cart at a
safe speed (40 is a good safe speed, 60 is riskier but if you derail you
can put the cart back on the rails), until you reach the [rails with
holes](Traps#Rails_With_Holes "rails with holes"){.wikilink} in them
where you should slow down, but if you derail, don\'t worry because you
can put the cart back on the rails. You should bring a passenger cart or
another way to transport a car battery for the next segment, but it
isn\'t necessary. Throw crashed carts or debris off the rails if it
blocks your path.

If you want to save fuel for the next leg of the track and are sure that
you can do it, simply carry the cart along the rails until you reach
bill\'s batteries.

### [Bill\'s Batteries](Bill's_Batteries "Bill's Batteries"){.wikilink} to [Cart Dealership](Cart_Dealership "Cart Dealership"){.wikilink} {#bills_batteries_to_cart_dealership}

![A close up of Bill\'s
Batteries.](Bills.png "A close up of Bill's Batteries.") If you brought
a second cart or passenger cart, simply place the rusty car battery,
found in the dumpster on the left side of Bill\'s Batteries in the cart
and continue. If you forgot to or neglected to bring a cart, you can
attach a rusty used passenger cart to your cart to carry the battery,
however it is unreliable, and it is recommended to bring a normal
passenger cart. It isn\'t strictly necessary to refuel here, but it is a
good idea. To refuel, simply connect your cart to the rusty passenger
cart, then disconnect it and throw the rusty cart off. Repeat until the
fuel tank is (sufficiently) full. If you lose your cart here, you can
get a rusty cart, but it\'s a better idea to walk back to spawn or
reset. The rusty carts cannot be stopped with the start/stop button, are
less stable and are generally unreliable. You need to speed up to speeds
of around 100 to get up the slope with a car battery, which is quite
heavy. After passing the slope, there is a tunnel which doesn\'t do
anything, and you just need to maintain a safe speed until you reach the
[clothes line](Traps#Clothes_Lines "clothes line"){.wikilink}.

If you have [glue](glue "glue"){.wikilink} or the stapler, simply set
the battery upright in your cart and glue it in. Since there is no
passenger cart you can get up the hill at a more safe speed, and have
less risk of derailing or tipping over while doing so.

The clothes line can be passed in several ways, such as slowing down to
speeds of around 20 and jumping over the clothes line, getting out of
the cart and walking under the clothes line, standing on the line as the
cart goes by and jumping in or any other method that works. Your car
battery shouldn\'t get caught on the line.

After the clothes line is the first occurrence of [bumpy
rails](Traps#Bumpy_Rails "bumpy rails"){.wikilink}, which you should go
over of speeds of around 30. The final obstacle of this segment is [1000
degree hot
rails](Traps#1000_Degree_Rails "1000 degree hot rails"){.wikilink},
which are there to prevent people from walking the whole way. 1000
degree hot rails are very easy to spot as they are neon orange, but they
don\'t damage your cart and you should be fine unless you touch them, so
I won\'t mention every occurrence except when it is useful information.

### [Cart Dealership](Cart_Dealership "Cart Dealership"){.wikilink} Information {#cart_dealership_information}

![A close up of Cart
Dealership.](Dealership.png "A close up of Cart Dealership.") The cart
dealership has 2 luxury cart types, which are the UltraCart™ EX
SUPERGRIP TRACTION SINGLE-SEAT RaceCart and the CartMaster Deluxe
Luxury® w/Kitchen. The UltraCart™ has many features, listed on the
[Carts](Carts "Carts"){.wikilink} page, the most notable being the
rockets to go very fast. The CartMaster® Deluxe doesn\'t have any
notable benefits except having 2 seats and the speed buttons changing
the speed by 2 at a time, which could be seen as a downside. The carts
will run out of fuel before reaching the Petrol Station, so they are not
recommended. You can buy a luxury car battery here for 20 robux, but in
my opinion it isn\'t worth 20 robux[^1] to save time respawning and
getting a car battery this early. It\'s your money though.

### Cart Dealership to Petrol Station {#cart_dealership_to_petrol_station}

You should refuel in the same way that you refuelled at Bill\'s
Batteries here, so you don\'t run out. To progress past the large blast
door, insert the car battery into the slot with a large sign saying
\"battery goes here\". It will take some time to fit it in, but have the
cart ready on the tracks as the blast door opens and closes very
quickly, and the car battery will fly out of the slot and become
unusable if it doesn\'t fall into the void after use. There is a ramp
leading up to a steep drop with a [gun
trap](Traps#Gun_Traps "gun trap"){.wikilink} at the bottom.

If you have a component machine, anchor the battery by attaching
[spikes](Spikes "spikes"){.wikilink} to the wall with a button, and
using a rope or cable with one end attached to the spikes and the other
attached to the battery. This allows the battery to be reused to get
through [the gate](Traps#The_Gate "the gate"){.wikilink} without
bringing another one each time you need to enter it.

You can pass the gun trap in 2 main ways: going at 60 speed or higher to
beat the gun trap (keep in mind 60 is barely enough for a cart with no
extra carts behind it), or go down the slope slowly and quickly reverse
to avoid running into the tripwire so that you can trigger it with
something and pass safely. You can safely touch the wire if you
immediately jump away. The gun will go off and go through a lengthy
reload time. Drive through while the gun is reloading (indicated by
smoke coming out the end.[^2] There is then a stretch of track where the
only obstacle is 1000 degree hot rails, followed by a [piano
trap](Traps#Piano_Trap "piano trap"){.wikilink}.

You can pass the piano trap by going at around 60 speed, but a good way
to get through is to touch the tripwire at speed 5 and quickly reverse,
then drive ahead before the piano respawns. After the piano is the
[moving platform](Traps#Moving_Platform "moving platform"){.wikilink},
which depending on server lag amounts may require you to drive forwards
at low speeds to prevent falling. The emergency button explodes the
platform, so press it if the platform is bugged. After the moving
platform, there is a [wrecking
ball](Traps#Wrecking_Ball "wrecking ball"){.wikilink}. The wrecking ball
swings 3 times before resting for a short time. Advance during this rest
period. After the wrecking ball is the
[lift](Traps#Lift "lift"){.wikilink} which takes you to the petrol
station. You can stay in the cart when you press the lift call button.
When the lift arrives, drive into it at around 20 speed. Do not press
the alarm button, as it drops you into the lift shaft where you must
reset. Press the button to go to floor 2 and put your cart onto the
rails there. If the doors close, you may have to press 1 again then 2
again to reopen them.

### [Petrol Station](Petrol_Station "Petrol Station"){.wikilink} Information {#petrol_station_information}

![A close up of the Gas
station.](Gas_station.png "A close up of the Gas station.") The petrol
station has a lot of hidden things (although they are not hard to find
if you know where to look). You can visit the page to learn more about
these. The only things you need to know about it if you are just trying
to reach the end is that despite what the tall person says, there is a
working fuel pump inside the main building, and you can also refuel with
a large fuel tank or take it with you, although you don\'t need to do
this for multiple reasons. Firstly, a full tank of fuel in a standard
cart can get you to the end of the game. Secondly, even if you are
attempting something else, such as making golden lemonade or summoning
Riel, you will probably have already beaten the game, which gives you a
jerrycan to refuel with at any point.

### Petrol Station to [Cathedral](Cathedral "Cathedral"){.wikilink} {#petrol_station_to_cathedral}

The way between the petrol station and cathedral is the most dangerous
section of the game. It starts off relatively safe, with an [oil
trap](Traps#Oil_Trap "oil trap"){.wikilink} that you don\'t even need to
reverse after touching the tripwire, just to stop, and some bumpy rails.

After this, several unique traps are introduced. There is a [dart
trap](Traps#Dart_Trap "dart trap"){.wikilink} which shoots darts at your
cart, which knock carts off of the rails into the void. You can bypass
it by triggering it without being in the way as described earlier for
the gun trap. After the dart trap is a gun trap, which I have already
given instructions for passing. However, I do not recommend passing it
with speed here, as there is a [banana
peel](Traps#Banana_Peels "banana peel"){.wikilink} afterwards.

Somehow, touching the banana peel is incredibly lethal. The banana peel
can fling your cart off of the rails and into the void, ruining your
entire run. It is also the safest to bypass in my opinion. You simply
have to get in grabbing range and throw it off the edge. I recommend
doing this in first person while in the cart for safety, as third person
grabbing can result in the object moving a large distance, possibly into
you.

After this is a bend in the track made of 1000 degree hot rails, which
is in range of the [speakers](Traps#Speaker "speakers"){.wikilink}. You
can get through normally, but I recommend driving through at a safe
speed while standing on the controls instead of sitting. The reason is
that the speakers only affect the player, not the cart and can derail
the cart if occupied, but barely affect the player. Following an upwards
area, there is another clothes line, which I have already told you how
to pass.

After the clothes line, the speakers are a much bigger danger. You
should stand on the controls of the cart and go at around 25 speed,
especially if you are ferrying things like the lemon or the screw for
achievements without securing them as a derailment without falling still
risks losing the item. After the speakers is the final real threat other
than players. There is a banana peel on the 1000 degree hot rails, which
both disguises the yellow peel against the orange rail, and makes
removal harder if you prefer to dismount the cart to do so. After the
banana peel is some bumpy rails and ramps. By this point, you are
probably safe. However, there is a [large
cannon](Traps#Cannon "large cannon"){.wikilink} which shoots bursts of 4
explosive projectiles. The cannon is not automated, and requires a
player to use. I have never seen anyone use the cannon, but if someone
is there who knows how to use it, you are probably cooked as it has a
low cooldown and explosive ammo.[^3]

### End of the journey {#end_of_the_journey}

![A close up of the
Cathedral.](Cathedral_cdrg.png "A close up of the Cathedral.") Once you
reach the cathedral land, you are safe from traps. However, you are not
safe from players, although most people at the cathedral will be badge
hunting, fishing, building or trying to win themselves. To proceed,
simply walk into the portal inside the building. You will teleport to
another game.

### Final step {#final_step}

After going through the portal, you will be taken to the Hall of the
[Cart God](Cart_God "Cart God"){.wikilink}. You must ride a cart down a
straight track to reach the Cart God\'s chamber. You have 20 lives, and
while that may seem like a lot, don\'t waste them. The Cart God is
difficult for new players.[^4] You must dodge the Cart God\'s attacks
until it throws a fuel tanker at you. You are meant to throw it back at
the Cart God, but an easier method is to carry it to the back of the
chamber and touch it to the Cart God\'s ankles. The explosion will not
damage you. If you are worried about the second and third phase, let
cart god fill the arena with fuel tankers, and he will damage himself as
he drifts around, letting you focus on dodging the attacks rather than
bringing the fuel tankers to him. The Cart God will enter a second phase
after being hit once with harder, but still reasonable to dodge attacks,
and upon a second hit, will enter a final phase which is very difficult
and you must use shift lock to face yourself parallel to its attacks to
dodge them. After getting a third hit, the Cart God will collapse into a
black hole and you must jump into the pit, which will have become safe.
I\'m not going to spoil the ending for you, even though I have told you
about the bossfight already.
