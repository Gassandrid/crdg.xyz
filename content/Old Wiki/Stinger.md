The stinger is an admin weapon in CRDG. it appears to be a long green
tube.

## Function

Upon use, it fires a homing rocket towards the user\'s cursor. This
rocket will steer itself while flying and will almost always hit its
target once launched. Upon hitting a solid object or player, it
instantaneously causes an [Explosion](Explosion "Explosion"){.wikilink},
almost certainly killing the player and destroying any builds it hits.

It appears specifically designed to destroy flying machines, since
schnogrind, the owner of the game, does not like them. It does so very
effectively and moderators with it will likely shoot down any flying
machines they see. This can be countered with D30 plating from the
[Fab](Fab.md "printer"){.wikilink} although it is inconsistent.
