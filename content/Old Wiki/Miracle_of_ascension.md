## Overview

![The miracle of ascension gamepass
icon](Laddeerer.png "The miracle of ascension gamepass icon") The
miracle of ascension was a miracle available during the first black
friday for 1000 robux. This miracle gives you the ladder item which can
be placed anywhere on the map.

## Ladder

Whilst holding the ladder item a semi-transparent outline of the ladder
will appear in front of your character to show you where it will spawn
when you place it. Upon clicking, the ladder will be placed, clicking on
your ladder while its placed while holding the item will restore it. If
your ladder falls in the void or is destroyed and despawns it will also
automatically be restored so you can place it again.

The ladder can be used to skip segments of track, build, or even fling.

### Gas station ladder {#gas_station_ladder}

![The icon for the ladder
tool](Ladertool.png "The icon for the ladder tool") A smaller ladder
also spawns inside of the back room in the gas station, it is roughly
half the size of the ascension ladder.
