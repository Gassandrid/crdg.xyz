The SMLE is an admin weapon in CRDG. It resembles a real gun, the Lee
Enfield.

## Function

The SMLE files perfectly accurate bullets toward the cursor when used.
This does 36 damage on bodyshot and 53 on headshot at close range, but
the damage drops off with range. From cathedral to the spawn, it takes
roughly 18 headshots to kill a player.

It does need to reload, but it is a relatively quick one and when
fighting players without admin weapons you can easily kill them before
they kill you as long as you land your shots.
