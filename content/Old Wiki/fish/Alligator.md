`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the
Alligator in
game.](Alligator.png "A close up of the Alligator in game.") The
alligator is a reptile that can be obtained, it is characterized by
it\'s scaly skin and 4 limbs.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

Upon activation it will start walking to the nearest player and upon
contact with a limp it will start chewing it until it\'s gone and then
it just repeats the process until it\'s deactivated.
