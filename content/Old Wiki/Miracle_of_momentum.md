height="156"} [The miracle of
momentum](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/659163221/miracle-of-momentum)
is a miracle that allows the user to dash forward. It has been up for
sale on both [Black Fridays](Black_Friday "Black Fridays"){.wikilink},
with the first time costing 100 Robux and the second time costing 200
Robux. It is expected to return in the next [Black
Friday](Black_Friday "Black Friday"){.wikilink} event.

### Function

By pressing G, the user will be flung forward about 80 studs, with a
cooldown of approximately 8 seconds. It flings in the direction that the
player character is facing, not the direction of the camera. Momentum
does not work when the player is
[destabilized](Destabilization_system "destabilized"){.wikilink}, it
also destabilizes the player on use.

When used with the [makarov](makarov "makarov"){.wikilink}, the miracle
of momentum\'s fling can be made much stronger (albeit less accurate)
