Every island except for Bill\'s Batteries has an NPC. You can interact
with most of them, either with dialogue or some sort of action. This
list does not include bosses. For the bosses, click
[here](Bosses "here"){.wikilink}.

## NPCS

### Crazy Jim {#crazy_jim}

Crazy Jim is located on the [Spawn](Spawn "Spawn"){.wikilink} island,
behind the right group only room **when in a [private
server](private_server "private server"){.wikilink}**. When talking to
him, he gives options for modifiers. These modifiers include hardcore
mode, making all rails 1000 degrees, disabling persistent tools and
entering building mode.

### Screaming Man {#screaming_man}

Screaming Man is located on the [Cart
Dealership](Cart_Dealership "Cart Dealership"){.wikilink} island, behind
the dealership. To get the alternative ending you need to shoot his head
off with a [Flintlock](content/Old%20Wiki/Flintlock.md "Flintlock"){.wikilink}.

### Tall Person {#tall_person}

Tall Person is located on the [Petrol
Station](Petrol_Station "Petrol Station"){.wikilink} island, near the
petrol pumps. He has no real use.

### Senile Old Man {#senile_old_man}

Senile Old Man is located on the
[Cathedral](Cathedral "Cathedral"){.wikilink} island, behind the portal,
on a slope. He talks about the [Cart
God](Cart_God "Cart God"){.wikilink}.

### [Poseidon](Poseidon "Poseidon"){.wikilink}

Poseidon is a [fishable](Fish.md "fishable"){.wikilink} NPC. By talking to
him, you can unlock the [](Fish.md#Miracle_of_the_ocean){.wikilink}, as
well as the Troutstanding Catch [Badge](Badges "Badge"){.wikilink}.
