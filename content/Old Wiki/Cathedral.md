The Cathedral is a big milestone in the game. It is the 6th stage of the
game. It has a portal that leads you to battle the Cart God. It also has
a turret that shoots cannon balls. There is also an
[NPC](NPCs "NPC"){.wikilink} at the back of cathedral that\'s called
[Senile Old Man](NPCs#Senile_Old_Man "Senile Old Man"){.wikilink} and he
speaks about the [Cart God](Cart_God "Cart God"){.wikilink}. There is
also a 8ball room that has a secret entrance. It has the most land, and
it is most used for builders. It is the only main island with nothing
that spawns [carts](carts "carts"){.wikilink} or
[trailers](trailers "trailers"){.wikilink}.
[Spawnoid](Spawnoid "Spawnoid"){.wikilink} presence is virtually none
unless they are delivered with flyers by other players, the main threat
here is other standard players.
