Marbles is an item in CRDG. it is a brown bag full of transparent,
colorful marbles.

## Function

On use, the bag is spilled all over the floor, disappearing from the
player\'s inventory and leaving colorful spheres made from glass all
over the ground. The marbles are very good at
[destabilizing](Destabilization_system "destabilizing"){.wikilink} and
tripping players attempting to walk on them. They despawn after a short
time.

They go away after you use them so they are mostly an annoyance,
although if used near the edge of an island or the rails they can trip
people off the map. They make a passable material for
[cardboard](cardboard "cardboard"){.wikilink} since there is a large
amount and unused ones despawn.
