`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the Red
Herring in game.](Herring.png "A close up of the Red Herring in game.")
The Red Herring is one of the many fish that can be obtained, It is
characterized by it\'s small red body.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

It does nothing when you have fished it up but when your reeling it up
it will disguise itself as a rare+ fish (ironically it can disguise
itself as a less valuable fish like an arapaima) but when you reel it in
closer it loses it\'s disguise.
