The main way of getting manifestation parts is by growing seed trees,
obtained by placing a seed in a planter, both from the component
machine. The seed\'s tree will drop some manifest items after growing
around 20 hours

The list below shows everything that drops from manifest trees, having
different pools depending on if you\'re in a private server or not.

- RAND gate
- NAND gate

- Diode

- Glass Pane
- Wireless signaler
- Attachment
- [Anti gravity device](Antigrav "Anti gravity device"){.wikilink}
