The Portable Cart is a [Cart](Carts "Cart"){.wikilink} in-game, obtained
by buying the portable cart gamepass. It appears to be a small platform
with attached wheels, a seat with a roblox noob in it whom is holding a
lever, and a fuel tank on the back. The noob can sometimes become a
classic zombie, Image to be added.

## Function

When the portable cart is set down it is removed from the inventory and
gives an onscreen GUI to the placer. this GUI allows the player to
control the speed of each side\'s wheels as well as whether the cart is
on. The NPC sitting on it occasionally makes baby laughing sounds.

If destroyed, so long as the fuel tank and wheels are intact the motors
in them still work, even if they are separated from the other parts.
this allows for onscreen controlled motors,

The cart is unable to stay on the track on it\'s own and must be steered
very carefully, as it lacks stabilizers unlike other carts. It has 45
fuel, so it will not last very long, and has low torque on it\'s
motors.![Portable Cart
gamepass](Portable_cart.png "Portable Cart gamepass")
