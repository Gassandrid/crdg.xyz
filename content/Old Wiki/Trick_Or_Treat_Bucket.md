The **Trick Or Treat bucket** was added in **Halloween 2024**. It could
be found at spawn and used to collect candy around the map. (candy found
at bills cart dealer and cathedral). After collecting a candy an image
of a skeleton would appear on the screen with the text \"you found an
candy\" appearing on the screen. After collecting all 3 candies around
the map you would appear transparent like a ghost with increased jump
boost and lower gravity.
