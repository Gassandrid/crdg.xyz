The flamethrower is a manifest component in CRDG.

## Function

When powered it spews a cone of fire forward, this blackens and destroys
parts. It can kill players in short order and anyone who survives is
still lit on fire, guaranteeing their death. It is useful for cleaning
off loose parts (eg, from explosions) and defending yourself at short
range if it is used carefully.
