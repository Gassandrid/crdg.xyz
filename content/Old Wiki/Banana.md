The banana is a component in CRDG. It appears to be the peel off of a
banana. appears identical to a Roblox gear of the same name.

## Function

The banana will fling any player or item touched to it a small distance
away, as well as causing
[destabilization](Destabilization_system "destabilization"){.wikilink}
to players. It will despawn after doing this once, so it is not an
effective weapon unless you are on the rails or you have many to spare.

It serves as a trap towards the end of the game. it is not very
threatening, being easily countered by grabbing it and taking it off the
track, but it can easily punish inattentive players or people going too
fast. Go slower on stretches with bananas to easily evade them. A
similar strategy can be used for bananas set on the track by other
players, although it may be more difficult if they are placed in
troublesome positions.

The banana can also be planted in a
[Planter](Planter.md "Planter"){.wikilink}. It grows much faster than other
plants and drops more banana peels, allowing large amounts to be amassed
easily.

## Trivia

Real life banana \"trees\" are actually a large type of herb.

Most commercially available bananas will not grow if planted. The plants
that grow them are duplicated via cloning (taking parts of the plant and
plating them in other places)

The peel is likely from a wild form of banana which does not have
sterile seeds given that it is able to grow a tree.

Like in game, real life banana \"trees\" grow very quickly compared to
other commercial plants.
