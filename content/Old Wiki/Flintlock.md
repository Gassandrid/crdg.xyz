The **flintlock** is an item that is given for getting the
[Finality](Badges "Finality"){.wikilink} badge. When used, it fires
rusty spheres that can destroy stuff.

## Function

Upon use, the flintlock fires rusty spheres toward the cursor with
minimal cooldown, which have collision and significant bullet drop. When
fired at objects, they will turn rusty after a few shots before breaking
any welds with objects it is in contact with after a few more hits. It
is often used in building, removing parts of carts, and overall
destroying things.

It is not very useful against players since it does no damage, but it
can [destabilize](Destabilization_system "destabilize"){.wikilink}
players on hit sometimes if fired at the feet, as well as being able to
knock people who have already fallen over around.

### Variants

Flintlock - exactly the same, but can spawn the [alternative
ending](Riel "alternative ending"){.wikilink}.

fintlog - exactly the same, but the barrel is floppy.

bananalock - shoots bananas that can trip players.

piranhalock - shoots activated [piranha](Fish.md "piranha"){.wikilink}s

pomnilock - shoots ghost pomni projectiles with no gravity that turn
people into pomni upon being hit. the shot sound is a slowed audio of a
polish man saying something

some sort of electric flintlock or blunderbuss - shoots electricity and
zaps anyone
