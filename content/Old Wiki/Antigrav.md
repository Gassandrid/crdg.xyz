The antigrav is a [manifest](Manifests "manifest"){.wikilink} component
in CRDG. It is a large metal ring studded with plastic, and is
obtainable in private servers.

## Function

When powered, the machine will create a black sphere inside of its ring
and heavily decrease the gravity of what it is connected to, often to
zero. They will easily make machines float away if they are small
enough. For larger machines it may require multiple to nullify gravity.

It can make simple flyers with one or two. They easily float away and
can be almost impossible to retrieve, even with flyers, so be careful.
As they are a rather valuable manifest, some people may detach and store
them if they have manifest as well. Only have them out if you are
actively using them, and don\'t take them out by random people. Keep
them away from [spawnoids](Spawnoid "spawnoids"){.wikilink} at all cost,
sometimes they will steal them or destroy them.
