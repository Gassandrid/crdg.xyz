## Overview

Black Friday is a yearly event that happens every Black Friday. On Black
Friday, some off-sale gamepasses called Miracles will go onsale or new
gamepasses will come out.

## Black Fridays {#black_fridays}

In the first Black Friday (11/24/23), 3 gamepasses showed, those being

  robux) (maybe will appear in the next black friday)
- [miracle of

  (600 robux) (maybe will appear in the next black friday)
