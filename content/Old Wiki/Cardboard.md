## `<u>`{=html}Description`</u>`{=html}

![Cardboard](CRDG_cardboard.webp "Cardboard"){width="138" height="138"}
Cardboard is a tool which you can spawn in a cardboard and use it for
building and stuff. to obtain more cardboard, you need click on 2 pieces
debris or limbs. To get it, you need to complete the \"[Delicious
Citrus](Badges "Delicious Citrus"){.wikilink}\" badge. Has two textures.
Cardboard can be cut with a [box
cutter](box_cutter "box cutter"){.wikilink}.
