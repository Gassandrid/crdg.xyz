The teeth pulsar is a multipart assembly. It pulses quickly to its base.

## Components

teeth x1

Tripwire x1

## Assembling

Set the tripwire on its side and glue the bottom of the teeth close to
the wire. Use the bottom of the teeth specifically or you may glue the
whole thing and make the teeth unable to clatter. If you have done it
right the top of the teeth should constantly flap through the tripwire
and activate it constantly.

## Operation

Glue it to something you need to receive constant pulses for it to work.
To shut it off press the red button to deactivate the tripwire, hit the
green button on the tripwire to turn it back on again. Be careful since
the deactivated tripwire will change its length, you may want to glue it
to a base such as cardboard.
