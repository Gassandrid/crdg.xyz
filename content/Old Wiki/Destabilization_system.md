The destabilization system is a major mechanic in CRDG.

## Function

The destabilization system governs when people fall over and lose
movement control, eg. if they were tripped by
[Marbles](content/Old%20Wiki/Marbles.md "Marbles"){.wikilink} or a
[Flintlock](content/Old%20Wiki/Flintlock.md "Flintlock"){.wikilink}. Most strong flings (eg,
from [banana peel](content/Old%20Wiki/Banana.md "banana peel"){.wikilink}) result in
destabilization. Short destabilizations are sometimes referred to as
being tripped.

It can be triggered in various ways. One of the main ones is when
falling from a distance onto an unanchored object, or being flung. It is
very important to manage and avoid destabilization while navigating the
rails as falling over will usually result in your death.

After being destabilized the player\'s avatar naturally attempts to
right itself by flipping quickly upright. If the attempt is successful
then the player regains movement control, but if not then the player
will simply flop violently. This can lead to very comedic situations
where the player jostles erratically for several seconds.
Destabilization with shift lock or first person causes the player to
shake erratically while knocked down. Righting can be made more
difficult if there are more slippy objects on the ground (eg, the player
is slipping in a patch of marbles) or the player\'s avatar is being
moved somehow else.
