![Cakerbaker](Cakerbaker.webp "Cakerbaker")The Cakerbaker was added to
CRDG to celebrate GraveGateKeeper\'s birthday. It is a machine that
produces cake.

## Functionality

The Cakerbaker turns debris into cake. Cakerbaker requires at least 5
debris to create a cake. Has a \"Cakeometer\" that fills up as you put
debris inside the cakerbaker, and is seemingly powered using a connected
diesel generator.

## Location

The cakerbaker is found on The Cakerbaker island, which you can access
through the Cart Dealership island. It is connected by a very long
track.

The cakerbaker island is usually not in game. To get cakerbaker island
you need to wait until the players birthday date in Roblox and enter the
game, and the island will spawn! Or player can use the command
;cakerbaker and the island and the cakerbaker will be created
