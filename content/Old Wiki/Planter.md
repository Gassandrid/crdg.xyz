The planter is a [component](Components "component"){.wikilink} in CRDG.
It is a ceramic pot with soil in it. [Seeds](Seed "Seeds"){.wikilink},
[lemons](Lemon "lemons"){.wikilink},
[bananas](Banana "bananas"){.wikilink}, and golden lemons can be planted
in them. They are also used by [Bees](Beekeeping "Bees"){.wikilink} to
produce honey, as they pollenate the trees. This does not make them grow
faster. Gold planters are created by melting
[gold](gold "gold"){.wikilink} into a planter, which accelerates growth.
Lemon trees have a unique trait of producing 1 golden fruit when planted
inside of a golden planter.

Plants will slowly grow once planted, starting out as sprouts before
taking their shape. After they reach a certain point they will begin
producing objects based on what kind of tree they are. After their
growth time has elapsed the tree will stop growing and producing
objects, with the exception of banana trees which continue dropping
bananas.

  tree          Planted object                             Growth time   Produced object
  ------------- ------------------------------------------ ------------- ---------------------
  normal tree   [seed](seed "seed"){.wikilink}             19-24 hours   Manifest components
  banana tree   banana peel                                1 hour        banana peels
  lemon tree    [lemon](lemon "lemon"){.wikilink}          1.5 hours     lemon
  Palm          [Moray eel](Fish "Moray eel"){.wikilink}   19 hours?     nothing

  : types of tree
