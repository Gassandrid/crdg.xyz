The jerry can is an item in CRDG. It is a red fuel canister, presumably
containing diesel.

## Usage

The jerrycan can do two main things: explode and fuel carts.

If slid across or repeatedly touched to the fuel tank of a cart, it will
slowly refuel it. This is almost a necessity for the alternate ending
and moving places without using tricks or skips.

If the jerrycan impacts something hard enough, or is powered, it will
instantaneously [explode](Explosion "explode"){.wikilink} and unweld
everything around it while making it appear rusty. This is its primary
use.
