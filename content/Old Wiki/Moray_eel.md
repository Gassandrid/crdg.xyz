`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the Moray
eel in game.](Morant.png "A close up of the Moray eel in game.") The
moray eel is a green variant of the electric eel and is one of the many
fish that can be obtained. It\'s characterized by it\'s long green body
and being able to grow into a palm tree.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

It can be planted into a planter to grow a palm tree, which currently
has no function in the game.
