## General

A fish made purely of gold, not to be mistaken for a golden floppy fish
or other type, added in the 2nd Fish Update

## Unique Traits {#unique_traits}

They have no scale variety, no bones, and one if not the only fish to
have no color variety. The fish also Emmitt a sparkling similar to the
golden tooth
