The PTRD-41 is an admin weapon. It is a large, heavy sniper with a
comically elongated barrel and a muzzle brake.

## Function

On use, it fires a perfectly accurate hitscan bullet towards the cursor.
This bullet destroys any part (other than the map itself) that it hits.
This means it can easily oneshot a player by hitting and deleting the
head or torso.

There are no limitations to what it can destroy other than that it
can\'t delete parts of the map itself.
