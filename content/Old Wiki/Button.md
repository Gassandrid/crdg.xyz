The button is a [component](Components "component"){.wikilink} in CRDG.
It is a gray slab with a cylinder on the top, it can come in many
colors.

## Function

When pressed, the button will power anything it is connected to. This
power can be blocked by a [rubber
slab](rubber_slab "rubber slab"){.wikilink}. It can also be used as a
[bearing](bearing "bearing"){.wikilink} because the circular part can
spin freely. Different color buttons play different music and make
