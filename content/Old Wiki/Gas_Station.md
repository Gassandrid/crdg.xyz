The Gas Station is the second last section of the game. It also serves
as a refuel section for your cart. At the Gas Station there are many
buildings. On the side there is the [Seeker](content/Old%20Wiki/Seeker.md "Seeker"){.wikilink}
and [Knife trap](Knife_trap "Knife trap"){.wikilink}. There\'s an
[NPC](NPCs "NPC"){.wikilink} called [Tall
Person](NPCs#Tall_Person "Tall Person"){.wikilink} near the Gas pumps.
There is also a vending machine that has almost no purpose other then
getting drinks from it, although it also gives you a now not so secret
item if you enter the code 255 into it. Inside the store, there was a
radioactive Garfield Mug that referenced the radioactive mug incident by
the crackling noises it produces. There is one fuel canister spawner to
fuel up with, there is also a fuel pump inside the store. The shelf
inside of the store spawns [](content/Fish/Fishing.md){.wikilink} every 3 minutes.

## Easter eggs {#easter_eggs}

- On top of the building there is a bag of sand that seems to reuse
  Robux/Tix bag programming that seems useless until you get
  [cardboard](cardboard "cardboard"){.wikilink}, which it can be used to
  generate debris to turn into cardboard.
- There is a room inside the building farthest from the lift containing
  a sofa, a [lamp](content/Old%20Wiki/Lamp.md "lamp"){.wikilink}, a paper towel dispenser, a
  small ladder and some money.
- There is a room above the shop area that contains the fishing rod with
  gold in it. To get in, you must shoot the back off of a chair and face
  the top against the wall so that you can sit in it and teleport
  inside. In order to get the gold out, you have to slam it against the
  wall until it phases through to the outside. To leave, do the same
  glitch.
- If you put 255 into the vending machine it gives you a block with a
  code on it.
- There is a decal of an angry squirrel on top of the gas station. (You
  can feed it seeds and it will eat them.)

## Trivia

- This island has the most secrets/easter eggs of any location in game
  (that we know of).
