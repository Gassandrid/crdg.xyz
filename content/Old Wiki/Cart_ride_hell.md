## Description

![Spawn of cart ride
hell](Spawn_cart_ride_hell.webp "Spawn of cart ride hell") All banned
players go there. It is a baseplate with a red sky. There is a portal at
the back, but it\'s made of cardboard. There\'s also an endless ride.
There\'s a merchant next to the endless ride. He sells unbans for every
time. Unban price = 50 \> 300 \> 800 \> 2000 \> no more unban. In the
corner of the map there is a rainbow jail (baby jail) in which the
"baby" is located. When you try to talk to him, he will say, \"im gotta
get out of here!!\". There is a trash can with the inscription \"car
battery disposal\". If you go into a trash can, will you teleport to the
ocean, which contains a lot of used batteries and dead fish. For some
reason, there is also a battery insert made of cardboard. There is also
an endless ride called \"cart ride of shame\". Each cart has a speed of
5 and cannot be changed. Located at the back middle of the map is an
image of a portal with the text \"portal to freedom\" which obviously
being an image does nothing upon touching it. Also, if you were not
banned and got here by error, the merchant will say \"hey you don\'t
seem to be banned. or there was an error. this may have been a mistake.
you can leave\".

### Ways to get there {#ways_to_get_there}

1.  Get banned (By admins or exploit)
2.  Get kicked in VIP server or just get kicked (this only bans you from
    the VIP server)
