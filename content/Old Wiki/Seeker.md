## Description

The Seeker, also known as the Eye or the Flying Eye, is a flying object.
The Seeker begins to levitate and chase the player if they attempt to
pick it up. The Seeker will not stop chasing the victim until they die,
and has collisions. It is practically impossible to operate carts with
it, as it will do it\'s best to stay directly infront of the player and
can easily knock carts off the rails.

## Location

The Seeker is placed behind the [Gas
Station](Gas_Station "Gas Station"){.wikilink}. The Seeker is guarded by
the [knife trap](knife_trap "knife trap"){.wikilink}. The trap is rather
easy to avoid if you are careful, so it isn\'t much of an obstacle.
