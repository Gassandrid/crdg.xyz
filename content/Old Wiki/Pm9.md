The PM9 is a homemade weapon from poopmadness (Poopmadness posted his
homemade weapon PM9 to Youtube and made a video for it, which went
viral, causing PM9 to become a meme) given to players by developers.

stats :

• 20 round magazines

• great accuracy

• has recoil like the [Makarov](content/Old%20Wiki/Makarov.md "Makarov"){.wikilink} (screen
shake)

• 13 shots to the body to kill / 9 head shots to kill \[8\~ damage with
body shot/ 12\~damage with head shot 1.5X multi\]
