# Glue

Glue is a gamepass used for sticking two parts (components) together and
conduct a Signal ![Gamepass for Glue](GLuepass.png "Gamepass for Glue")
![Two springs being held up by
Glue](2_springs_held_by_GLUE.png "Two springs being held up by Glue") ,
they are mostly used by builders to glue stuff together and they are one
of the most important parts. The [Component
Machine](Component_Machine "Component Machine"){.wikilink} is mostly
used w/glue.
