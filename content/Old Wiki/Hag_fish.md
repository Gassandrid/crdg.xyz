`<big>`{=html}**Description**`</big>`{=html} ![A close up of the Hag
fish in game.](Hag_fish.png "A close up of the Hag fish in game.") The
Hag fish is one of the many fish that can be obtained, it is
characterized by it\'s small long body and four fangs on it\'s face.

`<big>`{=html}**Function**`</big>`{=html}

It releases green liquid called hagfish slime. It can be put in a cup
and you can drink it. It\'s description say\'s \"it tastes like salty
egg whites\". This is a reference to hagfish producing slime to escape
predators or when threated in real life. This is not entirely realistic
ingame though, because real hagfish slime is not green.
