Vending machine items are unique [Natural
objects](:Category:Natural_Objects "Natural objects"){.wikilink} created
by the vending machine at the [Petrol
Station](Petrol_Station "Petrol Station"){.wikilink}. They are extracted
by pressing the buttons on the machine corresponding to the item.

## Function

Vending machine items have the unique function of being able to be
picked up - this means that if someone releases the item from the
vending machine it can be grabbed and put in the inventory. When
extracted they are rolling at a moderately fast speed and if undisturbed
they will naturally roll of the map on the other end of the island, but
if picked up and dropped by hitting backspace they will have no
velocity. They have strange hitboxes and roll around very well altough
they are rectangular prisms. All but the 255 item can be consumed in
some way.

They are one of the few items that cannot be grabbed with
[Spikes](content/Old%20Wiki/Spikes.md "spikes"){.wikilink}.
