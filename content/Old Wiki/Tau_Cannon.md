The Combine Overwiki page contains an image of the Tau Cannon in Half
Life and will give context that is not found anywhere ingame.

### Appearance

The Tau Cannon looks like a replica of the Tau Cannon from Half Life. It
shoots orange beams and looks very high tech

### Function

![A picture of the tau cannon as seen in
game](Tau_cannon.png "A picture of the tau cannon as seen in game") The
Tau Cannon allows the user to fly with high recoil, and also serves as a
weapon. It fires perfectly accurate orange beams at a decent fire rate
similar to that in Half Life. The tau particles deal a decent amount of
damage, around 50 damage for headshots and around 34 damage for body
shots, and there is 10 rounds.
