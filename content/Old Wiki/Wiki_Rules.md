## ARTICLES

1.  Pages should be proofread before publishing; grammar and spelling
    rules should be followed. The locale is standard English not found
    in the United States. (e.g. : colour instead of color). \*\*DO NOT
    CHANGE IN-GAME ITEMS OR LOCATIONS TITLES, FOR THOSE KEEP THE
    TITLE\*\*
2.  Formatting must be consistent.
3.  This is a English wiki.
4.  Misinformation shall not be spread.
5.  Do not add spam to articles.
6.  Follow Miraheze\'s policies. ([Content
    policy](https://meta.miraheze.org/wiki/Content_Policy))
7.  No NSFW content.
8.  Try to write a summary of your changes.
9.  Most importantly, avoid bias. Do not include such words as, like a,
    (comparison that is overly opinion based), I, personally, etc.
10. For a guide on how to format articles, refer to the [Manual of
    style](Manual_of_style "Manual of style"){.wikilink}
