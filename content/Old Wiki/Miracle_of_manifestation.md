![Icon of the manifest
gamepass.](Manifestpass.png "Icon of the manifest gamepass.") The
miracle of manifestation is one of the few miracles obtainable during
black friday. The gamepass had cost 600 robux when it originally came
out. The miracle gives you access to the manifest storage (accessable by
clicking the box icon in the top left corner of your screen next to boss
hub) which allows you to store and take out manifests that have a wide
arrange of uses.

### Replenishing Manifests {#replenishing_manifests}

Upon buying the manifest gamepass you are given a limited amount of each
of the manifests to start out with (excluding a few, may change in the
future) as of right now there are only certain ways you can replenish
manifests/gain more.

Option 1: Growing seed trees drops manifests when they hit a certain
threshold (19 hours in private servers, 24 hours in public servers).
Keep in mind that growing trees in privates drops less manifests as well
as not dropping certain manifests like 1911, flamethrower, astroturf,
gold teeth, and rockets at the cost of the trees growing faster.

Option 2: Fishing can yield all manifests including fireworks which
could only be obtained during new years, although it yields less overall
compared to growing trees.

Option 3: You can trade manifests or fish to get other manifests that
you need. You also can steal manifests or just find them lying around in
random places sometimes.
