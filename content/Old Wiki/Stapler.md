## How to get the stapler {#how_to_get_the_stapler}

Get the [Lemon](content/Old%20Wiki/Lemon.md "Lemon"){.wikilink} behind the Bill\'s Batteries,
then go to the Petrol Station, plant it and make a contraption that will
squeeze [lemons](content/Old%20Wiki/Lemon.md "lemons"){.wikilink}. Wait for it to grow (1.5)
hours

Cut the [lemons](content/Old%20Wiki/Lemon.md "lemons"){.wikilink} (you only need two lemons to
make a full glass) then squeeze the lemons over a glass. Drink the full
drink.
