The honeycomb is a natural object in CRDG. It appears to be many
tessellated yellow hexagons forming a sheet, with pits for honey to go
in.

## Function

When initially created by smashing a
[beehive](beehive "beehive"){.wikilink}, the honeycomb is useless while
the bees attack nearby players. After a while the bees will retreat to
the honeycomb and it will be usable.

The honeycomb is inert until it is assembled into a box made from
[Cardboard](Cardboard "Cardboard"){.wikilink}. It must be glued and have
at least 5 sides, with an opening for the bees to come out. When these
conditions are fulfilled it will begin releasing bees to nearby trees
growing from [planters](Planter.md "planters"){.wikilink}. The bees will go
between a few trees before flying back to the hive and filling a hexagon
with honey. There is almost infinite range, the bees will fly between
islands to reach planters.

There is a super rare chance for a mutated blue bee with 2 heads to
appear from a honeycomb. It is much faster than other bees. ![this is an
image of the blue
bee](Mutated_bee.png "this is an image of the blue bee")

Several honeycombs can be placed in one bee box and they will all
function.
