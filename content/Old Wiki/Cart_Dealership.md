The Cart Dealership is the main roadblock in the game requiring you to
get a battery. Inside the Cart Dealership building there\'s a premium
battery merchant if you forgot to get a [Rusty
Battery](Rusty_Battery "Rusty Battery"){.wikilink} from Bill\'s
Batteries. Premium batteries cost 20 Robux. There\'s also 2 cart
spawners which have the [UltraCart EX SUPERGRIP TRACTION SINGLE-SEAT
RaceCart
\[B2482](Carts#UltraCart_EX_SUPERGRIP_TRACTION_SINGLE-SEAT_RaceCart "UltraCart EX SUPERGRIP TRACTION SINGLE-SEAT RaceCart [B2482"){.wikilink}\]
and the [CartMaster Deluxe Luxury
w/Kitchen](Carts#Cartmaster_Deluxe_w/kitchen "CartMaster Deluxe Luxury w/Kitchen"){.wikilink}.

If you don't need fuel, it is not recommended to get a cart from the
dealership, because despite having extra features they consume fuel very
quickly and can't make the entire journey without extra.

Behind the dealership there\'s an [NPC](NPCs "NPC"){.wikilink} called
[Screaming Man](NPCs#Screaming_Man "Screaming Man"){.wikilink}, and his
only purpose is to activate the alternate ending by shooting his head
off with the [Flintlock](content/Old%20Wiki/Flintlock.md "Flintlock"){.wikilink}. There\'s
also a gate blocking the path. To open the gate, you to place a battery
in the slot. After doing this, the gate will open and after a moment the
gate will close again, throwing the battery in to the void, although he
battery can be saved by quickly taking it out putting it behind a wall.

You earn the \"HALF WAY!!!!!\" [Badge](Badges "Badge"){.wikilink} by
arriving there for the first time

## Tips

Although it is impractical to take the other carts from inside the
dealership, they still have a use. You can connect them to your cart,
which averages their fuel, and then disconnect it to increase your
cart\'s fuel if it\'s low.
