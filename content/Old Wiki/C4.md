C4 is an item in CRDG. It is a brown slab with a panel wired to it and
taped to the side.

## Function

The C4 initially is held in the player\'s hand. Clicking on any surface
causes an animation of pressing the buttons on the C4 while it beeps
before it is planted at the place selected. At this point the actual
tool in the player\'s hand is replaced by a remote detonator, which can
be pressed to trigger the C4, which
[explodes](Explosion "explodes"){.wikilink} with roughly the power of a
[jerry can](jerry_can "jerry can"){.wikilink}. There is no limit to the
range that the detonator works.

The c4 can be attached to your body to act as body armour (terrorism is
another way) The C4 is bullet proof to every gun that shoots bullets
(makarov, pm9, akm..)
