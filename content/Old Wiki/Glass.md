![glass cup (with water)](Glass.png "glass cup (with water)") The glass
can be found in a room behind the cash register at the [Petrol
Station](Petrol_Station "Petrol Station"){.wikilink}, you can enter this
room through glitching in or jumping through the door into the room. The
glass is a standard glass cup, there is 1 glass cup filled with water on
the table (the only way to get water). Empty glasses can be found in the
cabinet on the right above the table. The glass can be used to drink
various liquids, such as water, lemonade, golden lemonade, gasoline, and
hot chocolate. You can only pick up and drink from the glass when its
full to the brim. The glass can also be used to get pressing matter and
delicious citrus.
