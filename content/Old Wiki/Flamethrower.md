The flamethrower is an admin item/private server unlockable in CRDG,
obtained via being given by an admin or defeating
[Riel](Riel "Riel"){.wikilink} during christmas (private server only).
It is a green, gunlike device with a canister attached and a trigger.

## Function

Upon use, it shoots a cone of flame towards the user\'s aim. This cone
will char materials and eventually destroy them. Players attacked with
the flamethrower die quickly and catch on fire if they manage to
survive. It is designed to quickly and easily clean up parts and reduce
lag while providing a good way to defend the janitor using it from
[spawnoids](Spawnoid "spawnoids"){.wikilink} without stopping their job.

It was originally unlocked in public servers by the general public, but
due to being overpowered its natural unlock was restricted to private
servers.
