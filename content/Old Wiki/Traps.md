Throughout the game there are multiple different traps, some harder than
others.

## Traps

### Rails With Holes {#rails_with_holes}

The first trap[^1] is pretty easy to avoid, just go slower. If you move
too fast you might get derailed. You can also just skip it entirely by
climbing up to a higher level before the trap.

### Clothes Lines {#clothes_lines}

Clothes Lines can be passed in several ways, such as slowing down and
jumping over them, or getting out of the cart and walking under them. If
you don\'t move you\'ll probably get derailed.

### Bumpy Rails {#bumpy_rails}

For the bumpy rails[^2], just slow down. If you move too fast you might
get derailed.

### 1000 Degree Rails {#degree_rails}

1000 degree rails are meant to prevent people from walking the whole
way. 1000 degree rails don\'t damage your cart and you can just drive
over them.

### The Gate {#the_gate}

The gate[^3] can be opened temporarily by placing a [Rusty
Battery](Rusty_Battery "Rusty Battery"){.wikilink} or a premium battery
in the slot. The battery will be lost in the process unless there\'s a
wall stopping it from falling into the void.

### Gun Traps {#gun_traps}

The gun traps can be passed in 2 main ways: going at 65 speed or higher
to beat the gun trap, or going down the slope slowly to avoid running
into the tripwire, then baiting the tripwire by quickly walking in and
out of it. The gun will go off and reload for a while. Drive through
while the gun is reloading. If the bullet hits your cart, the cart will
crumble.

### Piano Trap {#piano_trap}

The piano trap can also be passed in 2 main ways: going at 65 speed or
higher to beat the piano trap, or baiting the tripwire by quickly
walking in and out of it, then driving through before the piano
respawns. If the piano trap hits your cart, the piano might fling it.

### Moving Platform {#moving_platform}

The moving platform[^4] is simple. Get on it and wait until you reach
the end. If you\'re lagging a lot you might need to drive forwards to
prevent falling. Also press the emergency button if the platform is
bugged, as it [explodes](Explosion "explodes"){.wikilink} the moving
platform.

### Wrecking Ball {#wrecking_ball}

The wrecking ball swings 3 times, before stopping for a moment. Drive
through when it\'s stopped. If the wrecking ball hits your cart, the
wrecking ball will fling it.

### Lift

The lift[^5] is also very simple. Open it, get in, press the button, go
out. To lower chances of items clipping through the ground, hold them in
the air.

### Oil Trap {#oil_trap}

For the oil trap, stop once you hit the tripwire. That\'s it. Wait for
the oil to stop pouring, then drive through it before the cooldown is
over. If the oil touches you, you\'ll probably die. If the oil touches
your cart, it becomes slippery[^6].

### Dart Trap {#dart_trap}

Same as the Oil Trap. If a dart touches your cart, the dart might fling
it.

### Banana Peels {#banana_peels}

The banana peels are small, so don\'t miss them. Get close enough to
grab it, go into first person to avoid it moving towards you, grab it,
and throw it off. If a banana peel touches your cart, the banana peel
will send it flying.

### Speaker

The speaker is intimidating, but easy. Jump on to your cart\'s controls,
and stay there until you pass it. Make sure you don\'t slip off your
cart though. The Speaker might derail your cart.

### Cannon

The cannon[^7] can be operated by players. Chances are no one is there
use it, but if someone is, then keep your distance, don\'t go forward
until they give up. The cannon can shoot
[explosive](Explosion "explosive"){.wikilink} bursts. If an explosive
round hits your cart, your cart will crumble.
