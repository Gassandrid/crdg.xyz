Trailers are an object in CRDG. They are similar to carts, but have no
engine and more attachment points. There are three kinds.

All trailers have two couplers, one on the front and one on the back, as
opposed to a cart\'s single attachment on the back. They can be chained
easily, although the more are chained the harder it will be for the
front cart to pull them all.

## Passenger cart {#passenger_cart}

Spawns on the [spawn](spawn "spawn"){.wikilink} island behind the
[carts](Carts#Standard_Cart "carts"){.wikilink}. The passenger cart is a
simple trailer with a similar design to the standard cart, but lacking a
sloped front or any controls. It has a second fuel tank which is full
upon spawning, allowing your cart to hold some extra fuel. Can be
chained easily to make long carts, fuel tank on the back is explosive
like a normal cart.

## Rusty passenger cart {#rusty_passenger_cart}

Spawns in [Bill\'s
Batteries](Bill's_Batteries "Bill's Batteries"){.wikilink}, next to the
[rusty carts](Carts#Rusty_Cart "rusty carts"){.wikilink}. Similar to a
normal passenger cart but is rusty. Wheel on the left and right are
slightly offset but it is negligible, still safe to use. holds the same
amount of fuel as a normal passenger cart but starts out a little under
half full.

## Fuel cart {#fuel_cart}

Spawns in the [gas station](Gas_Station "gas station"){.wikilink}, is a
large fuel tank on wheels. Taller then the passenger cart, has an
encased fuel tank. Very explosive if shot or thrown, will detonate very
easily. Holds a good amount of fuel inside, significantly extending the
distance a cart can travel without refueling. Used to spawn at the spawn
as well, but was removed due to
[Spawnoids](Spawnoid "Spawnoids"){.wikilink} using them to be extremely
destructive.
