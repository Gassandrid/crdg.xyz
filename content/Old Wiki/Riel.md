## `<u>`{=html}Description`</u>`{=html}

![Riel](Riel_boss.webp "Riel"){width="285" height="285"} Riel is the
second most difficult boss, being the finale of the Alternative ending.

## Stats

### Health

Riel takes about **50** damage from each sword swing (although this can
vary). Riel\'s health also multiplies based on how many players are in
the server when the fight begins. For example, If there is only one
player, Riel\'s health should be **50k**, if there are two players it
should be **75k**, three players: **100k,** four players: **110k**, and
five players: **120k**. If a player who defeated Riel enters, then Riel
will have **+100k** hp(this doesn\'t get multiplied by the 4th and 5th
player\'s health multiplier).

### Damage

Most of their attacks **instantly kill**, only some of his attacks being
continuous (so if you dodge at the wrong time, you only take a portion
of the damage.)

## Move set {#move_set}

### Teleport Slash {#teleport_slash}

As their first attack, they will teleport to you then slash, killing the
player **instantly** if not dodged. This is indicated by a sparkle on
the player\'s character.

### Continuous Beam {#continuous_beam}

For their second attack, they will do a continuous beam, indicated by a
red flash on his sword. The beam does continuous damage, so if not
dodged correctly, can hit you for some damage or even kill you.

### Ground Slam {#ground_slam}

After the continuous beam, Riel will do a ground slam indicated by Riel
facing your character. This ground slam also does continuous damage, so
it will do damage if not dodged correctly. The ground slam reaches
pretty far, so don\'t underestimate it at long range.

### Following Orbs {#following_orbs}

After the continuous beam (after they do the shockwave attack) Riel will
summon orbs above themselves. After a short delay they will fly at the
player(s). They do significant but not instant kill damage and are
evenly divided among all players present.

### Shockwave

This attack is after the Ground slam/Following orbs attack. Riel jumps
up and then slams down into the ground quickly creating a shockwave that
**instantly kills** any player that doesn\'t dodge it.

## Reward

Beating RIEL will give you the
[Flintlock](Flintlock "Flintlock"){.wikilink} item, the
"[Glory](Badges "Glory"){.wikilink}" Badge, the "Finality" Badge, the
[Marbles](Marbles "Marbles"){.wikilink} item, and access to fighting
[QROT](QROT "QROT"){.wikilink}. You also get the Ipecac sword in
bosshub.

## General tips {#general_tips}

Try to hit more than one of riel\'s body parts with one hit because each
one does a tick of damage.

You can do exactly two hits after the orb attack, two hits after the
shockwave, and two hits after the ground slam. This means you can
(safely) land about 8 hits per attack cycle.
