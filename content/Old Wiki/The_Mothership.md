## Overview

![](The_Mothership.png "The_Mothership.png")

The Mothership is a common name for the UFO that spawns in when a player
uses /e summon, and when there is no cooldown. The cooldown is around
15-20 minutes, although it is not known. The Mothership drops special
[alien items](Alien_Tech "alien items"){.wikilink}, like the alien
\[fishing\] rod, [Fab](Fab.md "Printer"){.wikilink}, plasma
shooter, etc. When the item is about to be dropped, the UFO spins. The
item then falls from the sky to the stage it is in.

Please note that this is different from the UFO that admins use to fly
around (those are smaller and are different coloured.)
