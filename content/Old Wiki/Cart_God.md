## Cart God {#cart_god}

The Cart God is the first boss in progression, being part of the normal
ending. Beating them will reward you with the winner
[Badge](Badges "Badge"){.wikilink} and the [Jerry
can](Jerry_can "Jerry can"){.wikilink}.

## Behaviour

The Cart God will only starting to move *after* you enter the room. The
Cart God\'s moving pattern is just staying in middle of the arena then
going near an edge.

Cart God\'s abilities:

• Throwing black/red orb.

• Throwing fuel tank.

• A laser burst (white/red).

• A powerful laser beam.

Upon dying the Cart God will get vacuumed by a black hole.

Then you jump down and \"beat the game\" by flipping the lever of a
generator.
