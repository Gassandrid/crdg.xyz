![Qrot](QROT.webp "Qrot") **QROT** is a boss which can be accessed after
beating RIEL in the Boss Hub.

## Info

- Entering the yellow portal in the Boss Hub teleports you to this boss
  after meeting the requirements of beating RIEL.
- QROT will be standing on one of the pillars around the map, sitting
  waiting for you, the player.
- Briefly after this, he will jump down onto the middle of the map,
  starting the fight. He is the 3rd boss to be added in the game, and is
  widely considered to be the hardest, due to the fact that QROT has 4
  attack patterns, double the amount of RIEL\'s, along with different
  attack timing per each pattern.
- QROT have 25k hp

QROT has only 4 moves `<small>`{=html}(excluding

2\. Leap → **2x** slap → **2x** dash

4\. Leap → Dash → Slap → **2x** dash → Orbs

## Notes

- A red aura will always encircle your player whenever he uses a leap.
  It slows your player down for around \~4 seconds.
- He will always leap to the middle after the end of a pattern. Every
  leap `<small>`{=html}(with the exception of the leap he does upon
  beginning the boss)`</small>`{=html} will be accompanied by 1--2
  pillars, which he will spawn in the middle of the map.
- Orbs are very likely to cause a ping spike. They\'re also very
  inconsistent.
- Dying does not kick you out of the game. There\'s a 20-second respawn
  timer.
- The pillars are virtually useless and serve no purpose except for
  starting different attack patterns.
- All of QROT\'s attacks almost kill you instantly
- You have only 4 lives
- if you beat QROT you get /e dansen and the Beat [QROT
  badge](Badges "QROT badge"){.wikilink}
- Please don\'t try to beat QROT it isn\'t worth it 🥀
