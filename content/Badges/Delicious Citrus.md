Similar to [[Pressing matter]] (this implies that you already know how to get this badge), you have to get the lemon out using 7 cables. After this you should get the knife and a golden bar. After you get everything needed you should head to the [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]] . At the [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]] you should head to furnace room hidden in the ladder and melt the golden bar. When golden bar starts leaking, you should immediately start pouring molten gold in the planter, but it is recommended to transform multiple planters into golden ones. After you get everything needed for the badge, start growing lemons, then harvest them and make golden lemonade out of the golden lemonades.

# Detailed guide 
### Lemon 

Lemon is hidden behind the Bill’s batteries ventilation. Next, build a simple contraption consisting of a Spike and exactly seven Cables. After you’re done, send all of the cables linked together into the vent and go in. You will need to glue the Lemon to the Cable placed on the floor, then detach the Spike and pull the Cables back up. If you did this step right, you should now have the lemon at the top of Bill’s batteries and can now move on to planting the Lemon. The cable placement for getting the Lemon.

### Golden bar 

**Golden bar** is located above shop in [[Gas Station]], if you have [[Pressing matter]] badge you can easily access it by glitching in by sitting on a seat with removed back, you still can get into the room if you do not own the badge, but it will be harder, but it is possible.

There is many ways to free golden bar from its room, but it is suggested to get 1 side of cable inside the room and the other end outside, or just glitch one end into the room by slamming it against the wall. After you and one end of the cable are inside the room you must glue the cable to the **Golden bar**  and glue the cable on the outside to a long straight line, or alternatively you could use a ladder in the [[Pressing matter]]  room instead by destroying every middle part and gluing two long parts together like in image below.

###  Knife

The **knife trap** is a trap found behind the main building in [[Gas Station]] and is the next step required to start producing **Lemonade**. Disarming it is incredibly risky, but the easiest method is to place an explosive (e.g. a fuel tank or a [[Jerry_can|Jerry Can]]) at the border of the island, shoot it and quickly grab the Knife. This method is best done with a **friend** but can still be done **solo**. Once the [[Knife]] detaches, you should now be able to grab it and move it anywhere.

> ![[Pasted image 20250830214540.png|300]]
> 
> An ideal [[Jerry_can|Jerry Can]] placement.

> [!Tip] Tip
> If you can, try placing a [[Planter]] close to you. If you accidentally get the explosive too close to you and shoot it, it wont kill you as [[Planter|Planters]] can block **explosion damage**.

### Golden Lemon Trees

**Golden Lemon Trees** are the next step into acquiring the **badge**. To create one, simply melt the golden bar into the planter, and then plant the [[Lemon]] you've gotten out of the **vent**. This will start the growing process of a **Golden lemon tree**, which will take about 40 minutes. During this time, you can building the lemonade extractor.

Once the **Golden Lemon Tree** is done growing, it will start producing [[Golden Lemon|Golden Lemons]]. To detach them off the tree, simply grab them.

> [!Warning] 
> **Liquid Gold** only drips for approximately 15 seconds, so make sure to make the most out of it. Such amount is enough for atleast 5 different planters which will speed up the progress greatly
### Glasses

[[Glass|Glasses]] are the main object to store liquid in. They are **required** for both **secret badges** and are one of the few objects able to be picked up and grabbable. **Glasses** appear at the [[Gas Station]] room to the right of the island and behind the [[Elevator]] and will respawn once you get them off. To store liquid in them, simply pour it on the ground and get the glass to touch the liquid. It should appear inside of the glass. **Glasses** are only able to be picked up if they are full on liquid, and filling them up with **lemonade** or **golden lemonade** and drinking it will give you the badge.
### Lemonade Pressurizer

The **lemonade pressurizer** is a **machine** able to crush **cut lemons** forcefully enough to squeeze out lemonade juice. There isn't a required machine, but the main way to make one is to use two linear actuators and hold the lemon in between to crush it. Once the crushing process starts, you should start to see lemonade spill out and the cut lemon becoming increasingly smaller. Once the cut lemons disappear, you should hopefully have a fourth of a [[Glass]] of lemonade. Make sure to store the liquid though, as liquid despawns in 30 seconds.

### Conclusion

You need at least **four cut golden lemon** pieces in order to produce a full glass of **Golden Lemonade**. Once you get a full glass of Golden Lemonade, grab it and hold left click to drink it entirely. You should now have the **delicious citrus** badge.

> [!Warning] Warning
> If you just walk into a glass of lemonade, you will lose a couple drops due to the equipping animation. Instead, **FIRST** hold an item and **THEN** pick up the glass. The equipping animation won't interfere and you should have the entire glass held.
