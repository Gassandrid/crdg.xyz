To obtain **Professional Gamer** Player must complete Super Hubert Man.

# Guide

To get **Professional Gamer** badge player has to spawn "**TV**" and "**Super Hubert Man**"  [[content/Collectables/index|collectibles]], it is obtainable thru [[Blimp]] shop. After connecting joystic to the TV player will be met with "**Super Hubert Man**" game. The game itself consists of 3 unique levels, and to get the badge player simply has to complete the game.

> [!Tip] 
> Badge will be awarded to the player that has entered the very last door that leads to victory screen, so you could theoretically get another player this badge!
