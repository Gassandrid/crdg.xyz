The **Derringer** is a **Combat**-type ranged **Weapon** added on August 20th, 2024 during the **bee update**. It is based on a real-life derringer. It deals an impressive **100 damage**, being able to **instantly kill a player**. This, combined with it's **short reloading** makes it seem like a good weapon. However, this isn't the case as the **Derringer**'s accuracy is the **worst in the game**, at about a radius of **5 studs**. This makes it almost impossible to shoot straight with it, and is only practically useful **close range**. This, combined with the fact it is a **one-ammo** weapon makes it one of the worst **weapons in the game**.

---

## Usage

Using **left click** while holding the **Derringer** will shoot out a singular bee to home in on someone. If the bee lands on someone, they will instantly die regardless if they own the [[bee repellent]] game pass.
