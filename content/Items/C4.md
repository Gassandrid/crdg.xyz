The **C4** is a **Combat**-type item added on **July 13th, 2022**. However, this item has been unobtainable since **January 29th, 2023** due to the [game pass](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/60559220/an-plantable-c4) you had to buy getting taken **off sale**. It is a placeable explosive that can be wirelessly activated at any time. Visually, it resembles a plastic C4. 

---
## Usage

By holding the item and holding left click, you will start a placing animation that lasts about a second. While you're in this animation, the spot where your C4 will be planted is shown in a semi-transparent green figure. Once it is placed, the **C4** appears in the spot showed and your item switches into a Trigger. Clicking once more, the C4 detonates and anything next to it explodes.

> [!Tip] Tips
> A **C4** can stick to anything, including players. Use this to your advantage by either planting it on yourself and diving into someone, or planting it or someone else. You are also able to stick it on unanchored objects, meaning placing the **C4** onto something light like [[Cardboard]] and throwing it acts like a timed [[Jerry_can|Jerry Can]].
