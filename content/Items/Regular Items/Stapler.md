## Usage

**Stapler** is a great equivalent to [[content/Items/Regular Items/Glue|Glue]], as they serve the same purpose of welding together unanchored objects. A stapler's staples are smaller and thinner compared to those of [[content/Items/Regular Items/Glue|Glue]], letting the user leave less mess. Although it doesn't have keybinds, making working with stapler a bit more annoying.
