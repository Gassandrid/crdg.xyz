**Marbles** were added on 3rd of December 2023, as a replacement of [[Flamethrower]] due to constant griefing and killing.

---
## Usage

Upon clicking on the screen player will use **Marbles**, opening the **Marble sack** and releasing them by rotating the sack 180 degrees and playing a sound. Upon usage player loses **Marbles** till he respawns. Sack will spawn 12 total marbles, with one of them being seemingly bigger. Upon touching **marble** player will be ragdolled, even if seated. Despawns after 80 seconds, gluing doesn't prevent the despawning.
