**Makarov** was added 13th of November, 2022 as a gamepass.

**Makarov** itself could be used for 2 different things, combat and movement. Pretty self explanatary weapon, upon equipping player will lose his forcefield and be able to shoot other players, pushing himself opposite direction of the shot.

---
## Combat

**Makarov** has 8 ammo, deals 15 damage and 22.5 (1.5x) if player has hit head. Has high recoil, aiming lowers the recoil and slighty lowers users fov.

---
## Movement

Upon shooting, **Makarov** will generate force, pushing the player in the opposite direction that he shot. This can be used for numerous things such as: boosting yourself up to reach heights, pushing forward to jump across gaps, and if you shoot while seated, the force will push all attached parts to the seats, as long as they are not spiked or anchored
