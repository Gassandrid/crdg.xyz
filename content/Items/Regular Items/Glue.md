**Glue** is an **Item** added on the July 31st, 2022. Visually, it resembles an Elmer's bottle of glue. There is a version of this item named [[Super Glue]], an exclusive item that is only restricted to moderators. It does not have an icon.

---

## Usage

Using left click while having your mouse in between two unanchored objects will create a glue blob that will connect two objects.

> [!Info] Useful keybinds
> **Shift + b** - will try to use glue on your current mouse position, **can be used without holding glue**
> 
> b - will try to remove glue based on your current mouse position, however, **you need to have glue equipped**
