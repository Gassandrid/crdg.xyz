The **Miracle of Lightning** is a multi-use Combat item added on August 17th, 2025. It is the reward for the [[Enlightning Experience]] badge. It uses two modes that are able to be switched together by pressing the E key or D-Pad Right button. These four abilities are themed around the shocking mechanic previously introduced by the [[Thunderbuss]], and were recycled to be used in a now-obtainable item.

## Abilities

**Abilities** are the main premise of the tool and they are mostly used for different purposes. They are contained inside of two modes, and inside of those two modes are two abilities. One of them is activated by pressing the F key or the B Button, and the other one is activated by left clicking or Right Trigger. These four abilities are themed around the shocking mechanic previously introduced by the [[Thunderbuss]], and were recycled to be used in a now-obtainable item.

### Shock Punch
*You kick into the air with a striking force.*
The **Shock Punch** ability is the first ability of the first mode and it has the fastest cooldown out of all four abilities, with pretty much none. It is activated by left clicking, and once used throws a punch that when a humanoid touches, will start electrocuting for about two seconds. This electrocution can be activated right after one finishes, allowing for a loop.

### Hover
*You use your arms to propel yourself out using energy.*
The **Hover** ability is the second ability of the first mode and it has the same cooldown as the **Shock Punch** ability. It is activated by the F key or B button and will start spraying out electricity out of your hands once you use it. This produces a slight boost in the air, and this can be paired by jumping to launch yourself pretty high. Pair it with diving, and you go insanely far. This can be pretty useful to reach long distance obstacles in a pretty short time.

### Power Push
*You shove into the air with a powerful force.*
The **Power Push** ability is the first ability of the second mode and it has a mediocre cooldown, finishing in at twelve seconds. It is activated by left clicking or the right trigger button, and will send foes flying with a sudden but deadly force. This is an extremely useful tool for combat, but can be difficult to use with it's lengthy cooldown.

### Direct Strike
*You point to the sky and command a lightning strike to the position you desire.*
The **Direct Strike** ability is the second ability of the second mode and it has the longest cooldown, clocking in at 85 seconds. It is activated by the F key or B Button, and is the most deadly out of every other ability. Once you activate it, your character will start pointing up and will order a lightning strike to the position of your mouse. This lightning strike acts like a natural one, except that it doesn't count towards obtaining the [[Enlightning Experience]] badge. This means that it electrocutes players an ungodly amount of time, allowing you to finish them quite easily or by stacking the other three abilities on them. This can also be countered using [[Lightning Rod|lightning rods]], as trying to strike an area while a [[lightning rod]] is nearby will automatically redirect it to the rod.

## Trivia

- Unlike most other items linked to a badge, this one was never shown to the public until it was obtained by a person to keep the surprise.
- This is the only item in the game to have multiple modes.
- This is the only obtainable "miracle of" item in the game.
