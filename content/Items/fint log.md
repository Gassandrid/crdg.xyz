The **fint log** is an **exclusive** **Combat**-type item. It is a joke version of the [[Flintlock]], having terrible accuracy, pitched sound effects and a wobbling barrel. It also has a longer cooldown than the original version, with about a fraction of a second as difference to make it even more inconsistent to use. It is based off a chat message where a user exclaims they desire the "fint log" item, which was a mispelling of the item at the time.
