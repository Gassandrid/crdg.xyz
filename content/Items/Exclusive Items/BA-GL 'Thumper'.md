The **BA-GL 'Thumper'** is an exclusive **Combat**-type item added on **October 22nd, 2022.** It is similar to a **Grenade Launcher**, both visually and usably. This weapon was directly ported from **Cleave**, an older game made by **Schnogrind**. Even with it's low ammo, it is able to be used quite fast by timing your R key presses. 

---

## Usage 

When left clicking, the **BA-GL 'Thumper'** will shoot out a grenade based on the position of the mouse. When the grenade lands, it explodes and can deal up to 30 damage if used point blank. However, the explosion done by the weapon itself is different from the explosions you see throughout the game (Jerry Can, Fuel Tank, etc.). This explosion can also knock-back objects by a small amount, and does not rust them.
