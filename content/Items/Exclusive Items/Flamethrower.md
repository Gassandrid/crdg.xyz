The **Flamethrower** is a **Combat**-type **Exclusive** item added on August 4th, 2022. It is similar to the [[Flamethrower|Flamethrower]] manifest component, but burns way faster and has a bigger flame. This item used to be given out to **Janitors** to specifically clear out debris, but was eventually removed.

---
## Usage

To use it, just hold left click and flames will start to go in the way your mouse is facing. Careful though, as you're able to burn yourself.
