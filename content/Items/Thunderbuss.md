The **Thunderbuss** is an Exclusive version of the [[Blunderbuss]] given to **THUNDERTOILET**. Visually, it is a retexture of said gun with a yellow neon color.

---

## Usage

The **Thunderbuss** shoots bolts of lightning that electrocute people and spasm out if hit by said bolts. While electrocuted, no form of momentum apart from the spasm applies to the player.
