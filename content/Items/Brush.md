The **Brush** is Exclusive item obtained by the holder of the "**Best Installation**" category in the Water Heater contest. It can be dropped, however when dropped it cannot be picked up again. Visually, the item resembles a wire brush. This item is essentially the opposite of the [[Touch of Tetanus]] item, cleaning things instead of rusting things.

---

## Usage

When you hover your mouse over a rusty part while equipping the **brush**, a blue outline will appear. Clicking on the rusty part will trigger a sound effect and a sparkling effect will appear. You can also clean rust by dropping the item and placing it onto a rusty surface. Once cleaned, the material will transform into metal.
