The **flintglock** is a **Combat**-type, **exclusive** item made on June 4th, 2022. It acts like a regular flintlock, but shoots out bullets way faster at about one per 0.1 seconds. Visually, it resembles a flintlock barrel with the handle of a [[Makarov]]. It is one of the only two semi-automatic guns in the game, the other being the [[content/Items/AKM|AKM]].

---
## Usage

To use it, hold down left click. It will automatically spray out a ton of bullet towards the direction of your mouse.
