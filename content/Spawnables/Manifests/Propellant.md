The **Propellant** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. It slightly resembles the [[Firework]] however it was introduced first in the game.  When activated, it creates an explosion effect that doesn't break parts or kill players.

---

## Usage

When activated you will be launched depending on what direction your are in from the propellant.  People often use this as a use of transportation or just flinging builds.
