The **Trigger** was once a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can acquired through **Fishing** in a public or planting seeds in a [[Planter]] in a public server and waiting 24 hours. However, it has since been removed due to a lot of glitches with it.

---

## Usage

The trigger was once used  to make your own items.  You were able to glue objects to the **Trigger** and pick it up. People would attach guns or other creative builds to it, and some even used it as a way to travel around the map. However, due to many glitches with the Trigger, it was eventually removed.
