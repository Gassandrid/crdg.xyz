The **Diode** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. Mostly used in tech builds.

---

## Usage

The **Diode** only allows signals to pass through in one direction. For example, if you place a button on the gray side and connect a horn to the red side, pressing the button will activate the horn. However, if the sides are reversed, the signal will not pass through. Players often use diodes in their builds to prevent signals from flowing backward.
