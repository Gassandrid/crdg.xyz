The **Wireless Signaler** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. It is essentially a cable without a limit.

---

## Usage

When activated, it transfers signals from one end to the other. For example, connecting one end to a button and the other to a horn will cause the horn to honk when the button is pressed. Players often use this in logic-based builds, such as drones and other contraptions.
