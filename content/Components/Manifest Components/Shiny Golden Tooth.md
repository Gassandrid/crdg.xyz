The **Shiny Golden Tooth** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can acquired through **Fishing** in a public or planting seeds in a [[Planter]] in a public server and waiting 24 hours. While it has little practical use beyond serving as a trade currency for players, it also gives off a sparkling effect.
