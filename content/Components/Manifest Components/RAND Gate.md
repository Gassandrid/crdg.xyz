The **name** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. These are used in logic builds.

---

## Usage

When activated, will use a `math.random` function to provide 2 random binary inputs ranging from `0` to `1`. Will also change to a random color.
