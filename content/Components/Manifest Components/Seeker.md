The **Seeker** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. It is a sort of a contained version of the floating eye from gas.

---

## Usage

When spawned, the **Seeker** immediately locks onto the nearest player. It has a long tracking range and also has an output on its back. For example, if a [[1911 Pistol]] is attached to the output, the **Seeker** will continuously fire at its target until the player its looking at is dead. Builders commonly use **Seekers** as turrets or even as makeshift gyro.
