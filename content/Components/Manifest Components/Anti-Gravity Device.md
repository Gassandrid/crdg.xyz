The **Anti-Gravity Device** is a [[content/Features/Manifests|Manifests]] item that can be acquired through **Fishing** or by planting seeds in the [[Planter]] and waiting 19 or 24 hours. When activated, it creates a black hole effect at its centre.

---

## Usage

When activated, the **Anti-Gravity Device** becomes weightless and begin to float. Many people use it as a means of transportation between islands, while others use its to put their islands into the sky, or even just lifting heavy builds.
