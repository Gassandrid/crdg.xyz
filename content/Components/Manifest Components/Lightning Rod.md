The **Lightning Rod** is a miscellaneous-type [[content/Components/Manifest Components/index|manifest component]] added on August 17th, 2025. It can be grown in private servers. It does not serve any important use, other than being a decent decoration. Despite popular belief, **lightning rods** do not count towards your global lightning strike counter, which is what led to it's downfall in popularity and usage.
