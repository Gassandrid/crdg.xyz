**Spikes** are a **Miscellaneous**-type **Regular Component** added on **May 15th, 2024**. Visually, it resembles a plastic brick with eight spikes under it. It is one of the few objects that can be glued to but are anchored.

## Usage

Activating **Spikes** will enlarge the eight small spikes underneath to a bigger version of the spikes. If **Spikes** are placed on an anchored surface, it will pierce through it and turn anchored itself. However, you are able to glue objects to it while in this state.
