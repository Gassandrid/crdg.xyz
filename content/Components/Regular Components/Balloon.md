The **Balloon** is a **Regular Component** added on **May 6th, 2023**, got removed then was added back temporarily on **July 29th, 2024**. However, the component has yet to be removed from the game. It is used to give less weight to builds, but is extremely hard to do so due to the anchor holding it off. 

> [!Tip] Tip
> If you use a [[Flamethrower]] to burn the **anchor** of the balloon to gold, it's weight will reduce due to the change in material and will start floating. This can be used to build flyers.
