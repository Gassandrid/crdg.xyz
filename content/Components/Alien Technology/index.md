**Alien Technology**, also referred to as **Alien Tech** are [[Components|components]][^1] added on November 28th, 2024 on Black Friday as the main feature for the [miracle of alien diplomacy](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/982093805/miracle-of-alien-diplomacy) game pass. Most alien technology are considered a huge step up from regular components due to their unique and revolutionary mechanics, but are also as annoying to obtain due to their repetitive and lengthy [[Alien Summoning|summon process]]. 











[^1]: Even though **Alien Technology** are unable to be stored and are somewhat difficult to get, they are still considered as [[Components|components]] due to how they function.
