The **Multidimensional Telecommunicator**, also known as the **Radio** is a **Miscellaneous**-type **Alien Technology** added on **November 28th, 2024**. It is mainly used as a form of communication in a "global chat", meaning that a message sent in one server can be broadcasted to another.
