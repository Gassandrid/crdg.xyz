The **Multidimensional Telecommunicator**, also known as the **Radio** is a removed **Miscellaneous**-type **Alien Technology** added on **November 28th, 2024**. It was mainly used as a form of communication in a "global chat", meaning that a message sent in one server can be broadcasted to another. 

---

## Usage

Like most other **Alien Technology** items, the Multidimensional Telecommunicator was mostly on the cosmetic side of uses, but also had some pretty niche uses, like being able to translate alien language (the language Martians speak) and turkey language (the language you speak after drinking the Turkey Potion).
