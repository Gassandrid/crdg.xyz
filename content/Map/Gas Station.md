## Brief description 
Gas station is 4th island in the game, and has many secrets. There is a hidden room with an open door that has 2 glasses and a wheel inside of it, then, there is a shop that has [[Regular Fishing Rod]] inside. Secret button under the trunk of a fuel carrier, secret room with sofa, lantern, ladder and a money stack, it is opens if player has [[Pressing matter]] badge and a secret room above shop with a golden bar inside

## Glass room
Glass room can be found in right wall from the shop door, there will be another open door that leads to the glass room. **Glass room** contains 2 glasses, 1 wheel and a smudge on the roof that will drip still water when raining.

## Golden bar room
Golden bar room is located right above shop's roof, and accessible thru glitching in using seat that is slammed directly in the wall 

>![[SeatExample.gif|250]]
>

## Shop
Shop has a **Fuel pump** and a [[Regular Fishing Rod]] inside, there is really nothing that unique about **shop**

## Secret button
There is a **secret button** hidden under the bumper of Gas carrier, pressing it will spawn a black part that can be seen across the map. Sometimes given part can start teleporting sequence, teleporting every player near that part to a randomly chosen spot from its list (It is currently unknown if every spot has been have discovered as of now)
