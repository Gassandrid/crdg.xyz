## Brief description 
**Spawn** is the first place and logically you spawn here, which sounds logically. It consists of a sign, group members only room, passenger cart spawner, cart spawner and a new cart spawner copied vertically, and has 1 total beehive spawn. Rails on **spawn** lead to [[Bill's batteries]]

## Beehive spots
There is a total of 1 **beehive** that can spawn on **spawn**. It can be found under the diesel generator at the corner of **spawn** platform.

## Group members only room
**Group members only room** has frying pan in it, you are able to grab it and smack other players with it *(frying pan is not a tool!)*, smacking players will result in **Team Fortress 2** Frying Pan hit sound and a short fling with ragdoll based on the friction of the hit.
