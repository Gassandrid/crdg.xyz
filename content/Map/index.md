CRDG has **5 main locations** on the path to completing the game, with their own unique features and materials.

## Beehive spots

There is a total of 9 beehive spots, it takes approximately 10 minutes for each to spawn, and drops from 1 to 3 bee combs on average, but there is a very slim chance (1/80, needs more research) that a **beehive** will drop 10-17 combs
>[!info]
>Note, that there is a limit of 2 beehives on a a map, and they can spawn in the same spot!
