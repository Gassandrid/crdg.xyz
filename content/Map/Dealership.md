## Brief description 
**Dealership** is **third island** and the halfway of the game, you even get awarded with [[HALF WAY!!!!!]] badge. **Dealership** itself contains **screaming man** (used for alternative ending, [[Finality]] badge), **thunderbolt siren**, **gate** that both can be powered using **battery** and dealership building with two carts inside, as well as salesman selling **premium batteries**

## Thunderbolt
**Thunderbolt siren** can be powered using **battery** (or alternative you could buy it from the dealership for robux), it acts as a megaphone when powered. Speaking into voice chat while holding walkie-talkie will result in a loud version of your voice playing from the siren

>[!Info]
>You can rotate the siren head to point at what direction the sound will play at

## Gate 
**Gate** can be opened using **battery** from [[Bill's batteries]] (or alternative you could buy it from the dealership for robux). Powering the gate will result in a short animation of gates opening. After approximately 10 seconds the gates will start to close

>[!warning]
>After battery has been used it will most likely be flinged in a fast upwards direction, it is recommended to glue battery to the [[Spikes|spike]] if you can. **(this also applies to thunderbolt siren!)**
