**Cathedral** is the last island in the game, it contains, secret furnace room, secret 8ball & 2ball room and a teleport to [[Cart God]] boss fight.

>
>**Cathedral** in-game

There is only 1 beehive spot, its located on the top right pole of the cathedral.

>**Beehive** on right pole of **cathedral**

## Blimp

[[Blimp]] has 50% to land on this island, the other island [[Blimp|blimp]] can land being [[Gas Station]]

## Furnace room
Furnace room is simply just a room with furnace in it. You can find it by looking at the right side of ladder, it has a slighty darker shade than usual. It is used for hatching [[Alien Egg|Alien Eggs]], heating up screw (for alternative ending,  [[Finality]] badge) and melting Golden bar (for [[Delicious Citrus]] badge).

>![[FurnaceRoomEntrance.png|220]]
>
>Entrance to furnace room.
## 8ball and 2ball room
This room is hidden closely to **furnace room**, sligtly closer to the first step on the ladder. 8ball is what you would expect and 2ball is a little bit tweaked 8ball.

>
>![[8BallRoomEntrance.png|220]]
>
>Entrance to the **8ball** room, marked by a cardboard.

>[!warning]
>Note that 8ball and 2ball do not respawn, meaning if you lost them or somebody else did you cannot get them on that server!
