**QROT** is the third and hardest [[index|Boss]] in the game. The boss is unlocked by defeating [[content/Bosses/RIEL|RIEL]] and obtaining the [[Glory]] and [[Finality]] badges. Due to it's difficulty, the rewards of the boss fight are purely cosmetic (except for the [[Cacsepi]]) and don't give anything useful. The bossfight was added on October 11th, 2022.
