The Cart God is the first boss pf the game, and is crucial to the games progression. To battle the cart god, you need to reach the end of the cart ride. You get teleported to a place, and there is a long, straight rail to cart god. You will run out of fuel around 3/4th of the way, so you have to walk using the cart as a flashlight. Once you reach the correct area, you should see a defeated warrior and a door. Enter the door. Once you enter, the Cart God will spawn. Cart God has multiple attacks, and you have 20 lives. To defeat the Cart God, you throw fuel tanks at him. You also need to dodge his attacks. Once you have defeated the Cart God, enter the pit of void in the middle. You fall, and there is a diesel generator. Turn it on, and you earn the badge and are awarded the jerry can.
