The **Cacsepi** is a **Boss Hub** exclusive **Sword** added on October 11th, 2022. It is unlocked by defeating [[QROT]] and it can only be used in [[QROT]]'s boss fight, meaning it is the only **Sword** in the game to be available inside of it's own boss fight. It is also extremely hard to land a hit with it, but deals major damage if done right. The design of it resembles a huge battle axe, meaning it is technically the only **Sword** in the game that isn't a **Sword**.
