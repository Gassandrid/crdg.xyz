The **Ipecac** is a **Boss Hub** exclusive **Sword** added on October 10th, 2022. It is unlocked by defeating [[content/Bosses/RIEL|RIEL]] and it can only be used in the **Boss Hub**. The sword is the same one used by [[content/Bosses/RIEL|RIEL]], and the design is a neon red **Sword** with a small frame holding the base together.
