## Description
**RIEL**, often referred to as **Riel** is a boss added on July 26th, 2022 and is often done as the second boss after [[Cart God]]. You have six lives inside of the boss fight, and losing all of them will kick you from the game for being "out of lives". Beating the boss awards the [[Flintlock]], [[Marbles]] (restricted to the [[Finality]]) badge), the [[Waypoint Teleporter]] (only in private servers), access to the [[QROT]] boss fight and the [[Ipecac]] sword inside of the Boss Hub. He has two attack patterns, which is less than [[QROT]] and [[Cart God]] but uses 9 attacks inside the attack patterns giving him more attacks than [[Cart God]].

## Attack Pattern
The attack pattern is always the same, and repeats indefinitely until the end of the fight:
- Teleport
- Red laser-beam
- Ground slam
- Stomp
- Teleport
- Red laser-beam
- Homing orbs
- Stomp
- Ground slam
  
> [!tip] Pro Tip!
> Riel temporarily pauses his attack cycle to walk towards players who have moved out of his range.

## Strategy
Much of the time spent fighting Riel will be dedicated solely to dodging its attacks. This is a long, drawn-out boss battle. Don't get greedy with your sword hits. Teleport and Stomp are instant-kill moves, while everything else is continuous damage; however, neglecting to dodge a continuous damage move can lead to instant death. 

Spend the first few iterations of your fight honing your dodging skills. If you can't dodge every single attack, that's fine - focus on dodging the insta-kill moves first, and tank some hits from the continuous-damage moves if you need to. Don't stand still during the fight: just moving out of range can eliminate the need to roll out of a Ground Slam, buying you time to get some hits in.

## Cheese Strategies
While it is *possible* to cheat, it is not often practical...

Using a well-timed macro to dodge for you seems to be an excellent strategy in theory. However, in practice, it becomes meaningless if the server lags behind even slightly: your perfect dodging streak, now stained by DEATH. Unless someone can create a macro that dodges based on Riel's visual cues, or has godlike ping, it is not feasible to macro against Riel's repetitive moveset.

When carrying a friend, you can tell them to climb the arches of the door from the inside the boss room, and Riel wont be able to teleport to them. Rather, it will teleport *below* them in the doorway. This way, you don't have to worry about who's being targeted. Keep in mind, however... the carried player is still not immune to homing orb attacks: they will still need to dodge and pay attention to Riel's attack pattern in order to survive.
