Gives player a [[content/Items/Portable cart|Portable cart]] item. When used, the cart will spawn next to the player and dissapear in his inventory. Alongside with cart, player will see a gui on his screen to control the cart, that has 6 total buttons: Close the gui (X), Increase or decrease left wheels speed, Increase or decrease right wheels speed and turn on/off the cart. Similiar to basic carts, [[content/Gamepasses/portable cart|portable cart]] portable cart has fuel tank, which can be fueled if needed.

More information can be found on [[content/Items/Portable cart|Portable cart]] page!
