Gives player Miracle of momentum, which is also known as "leap". Player can use leap by pressing G, as shown in the icon of said gamepass. 

> [!Info] 
> This game pass is only avaliable during black friday sale! Active from November 29 till December 3rd

---
### Usage

To activate the ability, player must press g (on pc), press on special button (on mobile) or hold both joystics at the same time (on console). This will fling the character forward.

>![[LeapExample.gif|300x300]]
>Player using miracle of momentum

> [!Tip]
> You can shoot makarov behind yourself in mid-fling to greatly boost yourself and vice versa. This can be used to skip major parts of the game!
