Gives player Miracle of ascension, which also is known as [[content/Items/Unobtainable Items/Ladder|Ladder]] item. Ladder itself is a modified 21-stud ladder taken from "Steep steps"

---

### Usage 

To spawn ladder player must simply equip [[content/Items/Unobtainable Items/Ladder|Ladder]] and click with his left mouse. Ladder will spawn right in front of him, and to remove it he has to click on ladder.
