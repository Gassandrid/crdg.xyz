**Dansen**, also known as **/e dansen** is an emote added on October 11th, 2022 alongside the [[crdg.xyz/content/Bosses/QROT|QROT]] bossfight. It is inspired by the [Caramelldansen music video](https://youtu.be/6-8E4Nirh9s?si=1XTM2mVATkeHn2a6), where the dance is shown.

---

## Obtainment

The emote is obtained through beating the [[crdg.xyz/content/Bosses/QROT|QROT]] bossfight, and is linked to the “QROT” badge. A guide to beating [[crdg.xyz/content/Bosses/QROT|QROT]] can be found in [[How 2 QROT]], however the page is a work in progress and isn’t finished.
