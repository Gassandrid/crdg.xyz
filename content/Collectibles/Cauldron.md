Once you have a cauldron you can create potions by adding in liquids and objects
> [!infobox|n-th]
Cauldron Once something has been added![[Pasted image 20250829024759.png]]

Now you can use recipes in the cauldron to make the Magic Potion in it give you an effect when consumed using a glass, mug, or water balloon.
