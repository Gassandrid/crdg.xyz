The **Blimp** is a feature added on **August 18th, 2025**. Whenever the time in-game reaches night or noon, the **Blimp** will come flying to either the [[Gas Station]] or [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]] and stay there for hours (or minutes depending on which cycle). 

> ![[Pasted image 20250822012557.png|400]]
>
> The **Blimp** staying stationary infront of the [[Gas Station]].

---
## Overview

When inside of the **Blimp**, you will be prompted by the owner, **Filbubar**. Along with him are three stands with a randomly selected decoration to buy honey with, the prices of them chosen by luck. After night ends, **Filbubar** will drive the blimp back off into the sky and restock once it comes back again, repeating a cycle.

## Features

The **Blimp** includes many mechanics and features to spice up the environment. They aren’t really useful, but are really funny to see.

### Defense System

According to a frame inside of the blimp, **NO** weapons are allowed. Holding a [[Flintlock]], a [[Makarov]], a [[Jerry_can|Jerry Can]], a [[content/Items/Flamethrower|Flamethrower]], a [[Tau Cannon]], an [[content/Items/AKM|AKM]], a [[Polymer SMLE]], a [[Menorah Sword]], a [[content/Items/PM9|PM9]], a [[content/Items/PTRD-41|PTRD-41]], a [[content/Items/SMLE|SMLE]], a [[Derringer]], a [[content/Items/C4|C4]] or a [[content/Items/Blunderbuss|Blunderbuss]] inside of the blimp will trigger the center room lamp to pop out a minigun and target the weapon holder until their death. You’re able to dodge the minigun from hitting you last second if you un-equip the gun the second it faces you.

### Decorations

**Decorations** are the main product of the blimp and are bought with glasses of Honey. To buy one, press the blue button on the depot under the stand you want. It will automatically drop out a basket of three (or six depending on the price of the item) glass slots that can be filled up by placing honey cups inside. Once you have enough honey to buy the item, simply place it ontop of the depot and it’ll open automatically.

> ![[Pasted image 20250822011710.png|300]]
>
> One of the three stands that you can buy **Decorations** with.
#### Contribution

**Contribution** is a system where **Decorations** can be given out to multiple players depending on how many ounces of honey they participated. Normally, if each player contributes one glass of honey per basket, each player should get the decoration. Contribution is given to whoever last touched a glass of honey. This means that if someone overrides a selection on a glass of honey, they’ll get the contribution even if they haven’t produced the honey.
##### Special Cases

- **Overfilling** can also be an issue in **Contribution**; if any additional glasses of honey are used to purchase a **Decoration**, the storeable version of the decoration will go to the holders of the glass with the most amount of honey in it.
	- If all honey glasses are equally filled, it will distribute by luck.

> [!Tip] Underfilling
> **Underfilling** can be used to efficiently save honey while also distributing decorations to other players. If the amount of honey in a basket is equal to the amount of honey required to purchase a decoration, it will accept no matter what. You can use this to your advantage and equally distribute the amount of honey required to buy into cups, meaning you and your friends are able to efficiently get multiple decorations.
