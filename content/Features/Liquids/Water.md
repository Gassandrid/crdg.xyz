**Water** is a miscellaneous-type [[content/Features/Liquids/index|Liquid]] added on June 23rd, 2024 during Summer Update pt. 1. It does not have any use. It is able to be produced by a [[Water Cooler]], but can also be obtained by taking the pre-filled glass of water at [[Gas Station]].

> [!Tip] Glass Transportation
> Just like [[Gasoline]], **Water** can also be used to fill up glasses really fast to transport them into different locations such as [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]].
