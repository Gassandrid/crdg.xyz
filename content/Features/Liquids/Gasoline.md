**Gasoline** is a [[content/Features/Liquids/index|Liquid]] added on June 23rd, 2024. It is produced by holding the filling station inside of the [[Gas Station]] building. Dropping **gasoline** onto a fuel tank will automatically fuel it by a tiny bit. However, this process can be done faster by simply using the filling station filler and pointing it facing a fuel tank. Gasoline is not used inside of any [[Cauldron|cauldron]] potion recipe, but drinking it while having the effects of the cart potion will speed you up.

> [!Tip] Glass Transportation
> Just like [[water]], **gasoline** can also be used to fill up glasses really fast to transport them into different locations such as [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]] by using the [[Waypoint Teleporter]].
