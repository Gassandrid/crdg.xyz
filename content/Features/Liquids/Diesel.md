**Diesel**, also known as **Gasoline** is a [[content/Features/Liquids/index|Liquid]] added on June 23rd, 2024. It is produced by holding the filling station inside of the [[Gas Station]] building. Dropping **diesel** onto a fuel tank will automatically fuel it by a tiny bit. However, this process can be done faster by simply using the filling station filler and pointing it facing a fuel tank. **Diesel** is used inside of the car potion [[Cauldron|cauldron]] recipe, and drinking more of it while the potion is active will make you faster.

> [!Tip] Glass Transportation
> Just like [[water]], **diesel** can also be used to fill up glasses really fast to transport them into different locations such as [[content/Places/Cathedral|Cathedral]] by using the [[Waypoint Teleporter]].
