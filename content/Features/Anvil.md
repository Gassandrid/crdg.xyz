The Anvil can be found in the furnace room of the Cathederal. It is used to combine/merge items together.
Anvil recipes:

**Jumbo Fishing Rod**
> `10x fishing rod`
> Catch time: 3 mins.

**Golden Fishing Rod**
> `gold bar + fishing rod`
> Catch time: 15 mins.

> `cog`
