**Manifests**, also known as **Miracle Items** are **Components** storable by the [miracle of manifestation](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/652433606/miracle-of-manifestation) game pass only available during Black Friday sales. 

---

## Information

There are a total of twenty (ninety excluding Fireworks) manifests, with the majority of them being able to be grown by **Seed Trees**. They serve different uses and are overall better than regular **Components** from the **Component Machine**. They are also able to be stored using the **miracle of manifestation** menu, opened by an icon in the **Roblox Menu**.

### Obtainment

There are three ways to obtain manifest components;
1. From the starter components you are given when buying the game pass,
2. From **Fishing** (considered a Legendary rarity),
3. From growing **Seed Trees**.

As of right now, you’re only able to get **Fireworks** from fishing, as growing trees won’t grow them and aren’t in the starter components you get.
