The **Poseidon's Rod** is a [[content/Fish/Fishing Rods/index|Fishing Rod]] added on October 4th, 2024. It was the first rod added to be able to be fished up at a Mythic rarity, with the [[Rainbow Rod]] being the second. Statistics wise, it is one of the best fishing rods in the game, having a cast time of about 30 seconds, 2 hooks and a luck better than the [[Regular Fishing Rod]]. It is also the only fishing rod in the game to be able to fish up a [[Rainbow Rod]], an upgraded version of this fishing rod.

---
## Pros and Cons

<p style="color: rgb(138, 191, 33)">Pros:</p>

- Due to it's performance, pairing it with other rods like the [[Rainbow Rod]] or the [[Alien Rod]] in a fishing net makes it even better.
- This fishing rod can be fished up by itself, meaning that getting multiple poseidon's rods or rainbow rods could lead to even more poseidon's rods getting fished up.
<p style="color: rgb(240, 27, 27)">Cons:</p>

- None.
