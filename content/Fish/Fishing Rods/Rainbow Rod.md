The **Rainbow Rod** is a [[content/Fish/Fishing Rods/index|Fishing Rod]] added on August 17th, 2025. It was the second rod added to be able to be fished up, with the [[Poseidon's Rod]] being the first. Statistics wise, it is the best fishing rod in the game, having the shortest cast time in the game, 4 hooks and a luck better than the [[Poseidon's Rod]]. However, due to it's insane performance, the fishing rod is only able to be fished up by itself or a [[Poseidon's Rod]] with a rarity of Legendary, making the fishing rod the hardest in the game to obtain. Like the [[Alien Rod]], an indicator appears on the rod when it has caught something, turning rainbow.

## Pros and Cons

<p style="color: rgb(138, 191, 33)">Pros:</p>

- Due to it's performance, pairing it with other rods like the [[Poseidon's Rod]] or the [[Alien Rod]] in a fishing net makes it even better.
- This fishing rod can be fished up by itself, meaning that getting multiple rainbow rods could lead to even more rainbow rods getting fished up.
