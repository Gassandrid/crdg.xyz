The **Regular Fishing Rod** is an object used to catch fish with. It was added on September 28th, 2024 during the Fish Update and was the first of it's kind. It spawns at the [[Gas Station]], specifically inside of the main building on a shelf. It is one of the only two fishing rods in the game to spawn around the map, with the other being the [[Rusty Fishing Rod]]. It takes five minutes to respawn. Due to it's simplicity, the statistics for it are pretty average compared to other rods, with only one hook and an average luck. It is also used as an ingredient in the [[Cauldron]]. However, it has no known recipes.

## Pros and Cons

<p style="color: rgb(138, 191, 33)">Pros:</p>

- You can easily automate a regular fishing rod farm using a tripwire and cannon.
	- This also means you are able to create regular fishing rods en masse, leading to fishers with an absurd amount of regular fishing rods.
	  
<p style="color: rgb(240, 27, 27)">Cons:</p>

- Compared to other fishing rods, it has one of the worst cast times in the game, only being behind the [[Rusty Fishing Rod]].
- This rod is pretty annoying to obtain in public servers due to the 5 minute cooldown spawn time.
