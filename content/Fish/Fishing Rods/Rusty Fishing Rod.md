The **Rusty Fishing Rod**, also known as the **Bill's Rod**, **Old Rod** or **Lightly Used Fishing Rod** is a [[content/Fish/Fishing Rods/index|Fishing Rod]] added on September 28th, 2024 being the second rod to get added. Due to it's rusty design and it's spawn location (being [[Bill's batteries]]), it is functionally considered the worst rod in the game, with the longest cast time and a terrible luck value. It also takes two times longer to spawn than a regular [[Regular Fishing Rod]], at a whopping 10 minutes. 

---

## Pros and Cons
<p style="color: rgb(138, 191, 33)">Pros:</p>

- You can effortlessly create an automatic rusty fishing rod farm using a [[leafblower]] and a cardboard wall.
	- However, due to the rusty fishing rod taking twice as longer as a regular fishing rod and it's placement being the second island in the game, it is very prone to griefing and inefficient in public servers.
- You are able to use this rod in automatic fishers as a delay tool to know when all of the other rods have finished casting due to it's slow cast time.

<p style="color: rgb(240, 27, 27)">Cons:</p>

- It's literally designed to be the worst rod in the game.
