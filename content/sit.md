**sit**, also known as **/e sit** was an **Emote** added on February 25th, 2024. Visually, it looks the exact same as the animation for sitting with an R6 character, however you aren’t tied to a seat by doing the emote. This is the only emote in the game without any looping animation.

---

## Obtainment

To get the **sit** emote, you have to buy the [/e sit](https://www.roblox.com/game-pass/721846412/e-sit) gamepass available for 80 Robux.
